Poetry

费扬 Yang Fei
<I don’t love you>
What is it to be like you? Till death we aren’t to be broken apart What is it the taste in your mouth? It says I don’t love you You are one of the ten substitutes What is it in your eyes you talk so loud but eyes barely blink It says lying While I’m lying in his bed
