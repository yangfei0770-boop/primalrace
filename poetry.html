Poetry

费扬 Yang Fei
<I’ll die after you>
The world is not dominated by posers Or human beings is gonna collapse Hope that get into these minions mind No it won’t They say You said life is about expression Aren’t we being loud enough Aren’t we laughing loud enough The whole country we have got fireworks They have got money Let’s make it boom Yeah it sure is the case Practically everybody can die without being useful Why not make it you Sacrifice being the big expression After you
✦
<entertainment>
The negative thoughts about me Is the negative reflection of yours When you bring out the sword Making bullshit in your toxic brains With the greatest imagination you can ever have While I’m laying here with my pure heart You told me what you are Clowns making a crowd Clowns are loud and obvious Real clowns never escape the fate of being Real entertainment In an absolute way Not in your mouth She tries to add a little bit of horrors He tries to add a little bit of lust Minions have millions of ways writing their own stories but they choose to suck anyway Being a collective entertainment Negative thoughts about me are just entertainment Wouldn’t you see it clearly? I’m all set
✦
<knights>
I come I protect I’m the violence itself She can’t see the problems And that’s why they are under our control Oh no She’s part of the problem She’s part of me I’m part of the problem, but Nobody knows As long as nobody knows Let’s do it when nobody knows What else would you do Wouldn’t you sing holy songs About our victories We are the problems they’ll never get rid of But let’s make em the problems
✦
<audience>
They need an audience I give them an audience Practical God is what they are making fun of Ungrateful bitches “But I’ve got power over you” Minions like you there are millions like you Can’t kill the roaches so roaches always wining But I forgive and love Practical God is what they are ditching against

<Knowing is suffering>
It would be 10 years since I left home I’m glad I met someone like you my friend It reminds me of a younger me I know why people love young energy now It is my turn to know now And knowing is suffering
✦
<current affair>
I’m not your face front you could use and dump I’m not your blood bag you could suck and leave I’m not your cloud daughter, dad, mother, son, go raise your own I have no idea what you are talking about

<overstimulated>
There are a lot of things don’t excite me anymore You’re not a saviour either The cruelest thing is to view somebody as hope Hope is cruelty Peace is the saviour They don’t know Should they know Somebody put the life weight on them once before Behind the back Who’s the saviour Saviour of what Life in confusion As the train goes everything seems normal as if it should be like that always We are all overstimulated But the end of tunnel is cruelty Because the trains have no stop At least that’s what I feel Will there be a stop?
✦
<sensitivity is a spam>
I touched the sky with zero confidence and I fell I lived on earth with much confidence but I’ve fallen from the sky So he doesn’t like me She doesn’t believe in me Everybody’s watching me They know what I’m thinking I know what they are thinking So she doesn’t like me He doesn’t believe in me Because I lived on earth with much confidence but I’ve fallen from the sky Tales going around saying how I touched the sky with zero confidence
✦
<bad vibe>
Stolen path or rightfully owned? It is me who have this life Blood stains all over the place I recommend you get your own life Every corner of the town There are marionettes who don’t know they smell so strong Because of the toxins inside of them Pouring out like invisible streams And streams unite together as an ocean And people drown And lost in it Bad vibes Avoid at all cost

<woman>
What is it like to be a woman Is that they fart a lot about your decisions Is that they choose who you are And help you because of their choice of who you are And bury you because of their choice of who you are Is that they want you to lose the voice Is that you are a specimen for them to investigate As long as you act your best interest And have a bit piece of war mind Which happens everyday In everyone What is it like to be a woman Is that they think they have the power over you Now shoot them in the head Show them who’s the boss
✦
<sweet>
Grass is greener on your side When you are in this middle of nowhere Watching stars and clouds move Dancing by the fire Under the night sky The grass is greener on your side And I wanna breathe the same air you breathe Because it tastes good like the fresh grass after the rain Finally I, celebrate

<I don’t love you>
What is it to be like you? Till death we aren’t to be broken apart What is it the taste in your mouth? It says I don’t love you You are one of the ten substitutes What is it in your eyes you talk so loud but eyes barely blink It says lying While I’m lying in his bed
