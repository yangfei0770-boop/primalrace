用核不扩散在沙特身上表演“特供”双标The Nuclear Non-Proliferation Scam: A Masterclass in Hypocrisy

国际 结构层 · 文化层 · 元暴力 The Guardian ↗ 2026-07-22 § 链接
核不扩散协议不是安全标准，而是大国用来分配特权的武器化叙事。
Nuclear non-proliferation is not a safety standard, but a weaponized narrative used by superpowers to allocate privilege.

这是一场典型的认知入口操纵。美国一边在伊朗战场上挥霍 375 亿美金，以“防止核扩散”为名将伊朗定义为全球威胁；一边转身与沙特签下 landmark deal，允许这个同样处于中东权力博弈中心的国家接触铀浓缩技术。这种极端的 contradiction 揭示了所谓“核安全标准”的本质：它从来不是一个客观的物理量，而是一把由权力中心掌控的尺子。如果你是共谋者，这把尺子叫“和平利用”；如果你是异己，它就叫“核威胁”。

从结构层来看，这笔交易的本质是资本与权力的共谋。Westinghouse 这种核电巨头通过政府背书，将“国家安全”包装成出口订单，把核技术的垄断权转化为高薪岗位和商业利润。在这种 masculine-centric narrative 中，所谓的“繁荣”与“安全”是建立在对特定地理区域进行差异化定价的基础上的。美国在输出技术的同时，实际上是在输出一种等级森严的准入制度，定义谁有资格成为“文明的核能拥有者”，谁必须被定义为“危险的非法持有者”。

最讽刺的 meta violence 在于，这种叙事被冠以“和平”之名。在同一个行政体系内，国防部长请求拨款去摧毁伊朗的浓缩能力，而能源部长在庆祝沙特的浓缩可能性。这种逻辑自洽的暴力在于，它通过定义“谁是盟友”直接抹杀了事实本身的危险性。当“安全”被武器化为一种商业特权时，真正的安全就成了一个 scam，而剩下的只有权力在棋盘上对原初种族和地缘弱势者的无情收割。

This is a textbook case of manipulating cognitive entry points. While the US spends $37.5 billion fighting Iran under the guise of "preventing nuclear proliferation," it signs a landmark deal with Saudi Arabia, granting them the potential to enrich uranium. This blatant contradiction reveals that "nuclear safety standards" are never objective physical constants; they are rulers held by the center of power. If you are a complicit ally, the ruler measures "peaceful use"; if you are the enemy, it measures "nuclear threat."

At the structural layer, this deal is a conspiracy of capital and power. Giants like Westinghouse use government endorsement to package "national security" as export orders, converting the monopoly of nuclear tech into high-paying jobs and profit. Within this masculine-centric narrative, "prosperity" and "security" are built upon a differential pricing of geopolitical zones. The US isn't just exporting technology; it is exporting a rigid hierarchy of access, defining who is a "civilized nuclear owner" and who is a "dangerous illegal possessor."

The ultimate meta-violence lies in the label of "peace." Within the same administration, the Secretary of Defense requests funds to destroy Iran's enrichment capabilities while the Secretary of Energy celebrates Saudi's enrichment potential. The violence of this logic is that it erases the actual danger by simply defining "who is an ally." When "security" is weaponized as a commercial privilege, actual safety becomes a scam, leaving only the cold calculation of power harvesting the Primal Race and geopolitical subordinates.
