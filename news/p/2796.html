以“自由”之名，将身体交给利益共谋的赌局In the Name of Freedom, Betting Your Body on a Complicit Gamble

国际 结构层 · 文化层 · 元暴力 The New York Times ↗ 2026-07-24 § 链接
当监管者成为获利者，科学就成了掩盖掠夺的文化武器。
When regulators become beneficiaries, science is weaponized to mask systemic plunder.

这是一场极其典型的、由权力顶层操纵的共谋局。RFK 把 FDA 的咨询委员会变成了自己的私人董事会——在 8 个新提名成员中，有 6 个本身就是这些实验性多肽（peptides）产品的卖家。这已经不是在讨论医疗政策，而是在进行一次赤裸裸的利益输送。当裁判员本身就是球员，且球员在兜售一种未经证实的、甚至曾被禁用的产品时，所谓的“放宽限制”不过是为资本开辟合法的收割通道。

在这个结构中，FDA 的科学家成了被牺牲的边缘叙事。他们试图用数据和风险评估来定义“事实”，但这种科学表达在 RFK 这种掌握认知入口的人面前毫无还手之力。RFK 并不关心多肽是否有效，他关心的是如何通过重新定义“自由”和“选择”，将这些产品从“研究级”洗白为“可销售级”。这是一种极其阴险的武器化叙事：将对身体健康的潜在风险，包装成是对“医疗垄断”的反抗，从而诱导受众在不知情的情况下参与一场以自身身体为筹码的博弈。

这又是典型的元暴力运作：男性中心权力结构通过操纵解释权，让一个充满风险的商业 scam 看起来像是一场关于“健康主权”的进步运动。在这种叙事下，被剥夺主体性的不再是某个特定种族，而是所有被承诺“脑健康”或“组织修复”的消费者。他们以为在选择最优解，实际上只是成为了权力共谋者们账单上的一个数字。

This is a textbook case of complicity orchestrated by the top tier of power. RFK has effectively turned the FDA's advisory committee into his private board of directors—out of eight new nominees, six are sellers of the very experimental peptides under review. This is no longer a debate over health policy; it is a naked transfer of interests. When the referee is the player, and that player is peddling unproven, previously banned products, "lifting restrictions" is merely a euphemism for clearing a legal path for capital to harvest the public.

In this structure, FDA scientists have become a marginalized narrative. They attempt to define "facts" through data and risk assessment, but such scientific expression is powerless against someone like RFK, who controls the cognitive entry points. RFK does not care if the peptides work; he cares about how to rebrand these products from "research grade" to "marketable" by redefining "freedom" and "choice." This is a sinister weaponization of narrative: framing potential health risks as a rebellion against "medical monopolies," thus inducing the public to gamble with their own bodies without knowing the odds.

This is the operation of meta-violence: a masculine-centric power structure manipulating the right of interpretation to make a commercial scam look like a progressive movement for "health sovereignty." In this narrative, those stripped of their subjectivity are no longer a specific race, but all consumers promised "brain health" or "tendon repair." They believe they are seeking an optimal expression of health, while in reality, they are merely digits on the balance sheets of the co-conspirators.
