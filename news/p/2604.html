蟑螂运动：在被剥夺主体性后的生存性博弈The Cockroach Movement: Existential Game After Subjectivity Deprivation

国际 文化层 · 元暴力 The Guardian ↗ 2026-07-20 § 链接
当一个群体的表达被定义为“害虫”，反向认同就是夺回解释权的战争。
When a group's expression is defined as 'vermin', reclaiming that label is a war for interpretative power.

印度这次的“蟑螂运动” (Cockroach movement) 是一场典型的存在性战争。在元暴力的叙事中，底层或被边缘化的群体往往被权力中心定义为“蟑螂”——意味着他们是肮脏的、低等的、需要被清理的，且具有极强的、令人厌恶的生存韧性。这种定义是文化层面的暴力，目的是通过剥夺对方的人格主体性，为随后的结构性压迫和直接暴力提供合法性。

但一个极具攻击性的转折发生了：抗争者不再试图通过扮演“文明人”来请求宽恕，而是直接接管了“蟑螂”这个标签。这是一种武器化的反向表达。当他们自称蟑螂并以此发起抗议时，他们实际上是在说：如果你们定义的真实是我们将我们视为害虫，那么我们就用这种“害虫”的韧性来摧毁你们的秩序。这不仅是对教育部长个人职位的挑战，更是对认知入口的强行夺取。

这种表达方式是结构性弱势者在绝境中的一种“假.最优解”向“真.最优解”的过渡。他们意识到，在既定的男本位、阶级本位的文明叙事里，他们永远无法通过“公正的表达”获得资源，因此他们选择用一种激进的、自我异化的身份认同，在公共空间制造一个无法被忽视的干扰项。这种博弈的残酷之处在于，他们必须先接受被物化，才能在物化的逻辑里反击。

India's "Cockroach movement" is a textbook case of an existential war. In the masculine-centric narrative of meta-violence, marginalized groups are often defined as 'cockroaches'—meaning they are filthy, inferior, and need to be exterminated, despite their resilience. This definition is cultural violence, designed to strip away the subject's agency and legitimize subsequent structural and direct violence.

However, a sharp tactical pivot has occurred: the protesters are no longer attempting to plea for mercy by performing the role of the 'civilized citizen'. Instead, they have hijacked the 'cockroach' label. This is the weaponization of expression. By identifying as cockroaches, they are declaring that if the power center defines their reality as vermin, they will use that very resilience to disrupt the order. This is not just a demand for a minister's resignation, but a forceful seizure of the cognitive entry point.

This strategy represents a transition from a 'fake optimal expression' to a 'true optimal expression' for the structurally disadvantaged. They have realized that within the established narratives of class and masculinity, they will never gain resources through 'just expressions'. Thus, they adopt a radical, self-alienating identity to create an unavoidable glitch in the public space. The cruelty of this game is that they must first accept being objectified before they can strike back using the logic of objectification.
