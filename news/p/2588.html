“无聊”的政治，是另一种形式的共谋The 'Boring' Politics: Another Form of Complicity

国际 结构层 · 文化层 · 元暴力 The New York Times ↗ 2026-07-20 § 链接
当政治被简化为“让东西变便宜”，它就在掩盖谁在通过昂贵地定义现实获利。
When politics is reduced to 'making stuff cost less,' it masks who profits from defining reality as expensive.

《纽约时报》在赞美 Roy Cooper 的“无聊” (boring) 和“朴实” (prosaic)。口号是 “Make Stuff Cost Less”，没有任何文化战争的戏剧性，没有性别议题，没有革命姿态。在作者看来，这是一种高效的、去极化的最优解表达。但从暴力三角来看，这种“无聊”恰恰是 structural violence 的高级掩体。

当政治话语被压缩到仅剩“物价”这个单一维度时，它实际上在进行一次大规模的认知入口清理。它告诉选民：生活质量的差额 (Potential − Actual) 仅仅是钱的问题，而不是资源分配制度、医疗准入或生育成本的结构性剥削。这种叙事把复杂的权力博弈简化为一种简单的超市购物清单。这种“无聊”本质上是对既定秩序的共谋 (complicity)——只要不触碰深层的权力结构，只要能让物价稍微下降，我们就维持这个一个男人定义规则、另一个男人执行管理、而女性在后台承担无偿养育代价的旧系统。

作者嘲笑“六个性别” (six genders) 这种戏剧性，将其视为干扰。但事实上，正是那些被定义为“戏剧性”的身份政治，才是试图撕开元暴力 (meta violence) 掩体的尝试。将政治“无聊化”是统治阶层最完美的武器化表达：它通过制造一种“常识性的温情”，诱导弱势群体放弃对主体性的追求，转而追求一个由共谋者承诺的、略微便宜的生存空间。

这场选举如果真的如此“无聊”地获胜，那它不是民主的胜利，而是 masculine-centric narrative 的一次成功维护。它证明了只要给人们足够的“低成本生活”幻象，他们就愿意继续在同一个被殖民的原初种族结构中安稳地睡去。

The New York Times celebrates Roy Cooper's 'boring' and 'prosaic' approach. His slogan, 'Make Stuff Cost Less,' avoids the drama of culture wars and gender identity. To the author, this is an optimal expression of effectiveness. But through the lens of the Violence Triangle, this 'boredom' is precisely the camouflage for structural violence.

When political discourse is compressed into the single dimension of 'cost,' it performs a massive clearing of the cognitive entry point. It tells voters that the gap between Potential and Actual is merely a matter of money, not a result of systemic resource maldistribution, healthcare barriers, or the structural exploitation of reproductive labor. This narrative reduces complex power struggles to a grocery list. Such 'boredom' is essentially a complicity with the established order—as long as the deep power structures remain untouched and prices drop slightly, we maintain a system where one man defines the rules, another manages them, and women bear the unpaid cost of nurturing in the background.

The author mocks 'six genders' as mere drama. In reality, it is exactly this 'dramatic' identity politics that attempts to tear through the veil of meta violence. 'Boring-ifying' politics is the most perfect weaponized expression of the ruling class: it creates a 'common-sense warmth' that tempts marginalized groups to abandon their quest for subjectivity in exchange for a slightly cheaper survival space promised by co-conspirators.

If this election is won through such 'boredom,' it is not a victory for democracy, but a successful maintenance of the masculine-centric narrative. It proves that as long as people are fed the illusion of 'low-cost living,' they are willing to remain asleep within the same structure of the Primal Race.
