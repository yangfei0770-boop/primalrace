特朗普的“漏洞”叙事：一场关于真实定义权的权力操演Trump's 'Vulnerability' Narrative: A Power Play for the Definition of Truth

国际 文化层 · 元暴力 The New York Times ↗ 2026-07-17 § 链接
权力不关心事实是否真实，只关心它能否被定义为真实。
Power doesn't care if a fact is true; it only cares if it can be defined as true.

这是一次典型的表达武器化操演。特朗普在演讲中宣称要“建立公众信心”，但实际操作却是通过密集投放“选举欺诈”和“系统漏洞”的叙事，在认知入口处直接制造恐慌。这种矛盾的逻辑正是武器化叙事的精髓：用一个正义的包裹（保护选举），递送一个破坏性的内核（瓦解信任）。

最荒诞的共谋在于，他甚至发布了官方文件来“支持”自己的观点，而这些文件在客观事实层面恰恰证明了选举系统的稳固。但这并不重要。在元暴力的逻辑里，文件的具体内容是次要的，而“总统发布了文件”这个动作本身被赋予了定义权。他并不需要文件证明漏洞存在，他只需要通过发布行为，制造一种“我在揭露真相”的视觉表型，从而在存在性战争中夺取对“什么是事实”的解释权。

这种“现实不配合就重新定义现实”的机制，与某些科技巨头宣布新定律来掩盖能力缺失的逻辑如出一辙。当 Actual（实际的选举安全）不符合其政治 Potential（通过质疑选举来合法化权力）时，他直接通过 Cultural Violence 将“安全”定义为“被操纵的假象”。

这场博弈的危险之处在于，它在诱导受众进入一种群体性的 psychosis。当人们习惯于通过权力的指认而非证据的推演来认知世界时，真相就成了可以被随意裁剪的布料。下次他定义谁是“敌人”时，同样不需要证据，只需要一次演讲和一份被歪曲的文件。

This is a textbook case of the weaponisation of expression. Trump claims his speech is about "building public confidence," yet he spends the majority of it undermining that very confidence. This contradiction is the essence of weaponized narratives: using a righteous wrapper (protecting elections) to deliver a destructive core (eroding trust).

The most absurd part of this complicity is the use of government documents. He publishes files to "support" his claims, even though the actual content of those files proves the electoral system is secure. But the content is irrelevant. In the logic of meta-violence, the specific data is secondary to the act of publishing. He doesn't need the documents to prove vulnerabilities; he only needs the performance of "revealing the truth" to seize the power of interpretation in this existential war.

This mechanism of "redefining reality when reality doesn't cooperate" is identical to how some tech giants announce new "laws" to mask their failure to innovate. When the Actual (electoral security) doesn't align with his political Potential (legitimizing power by questioning the system), he employs cultural violence to redefine "security" as a "manipulated illusion."

The danger here is the induction of a collective psychosis. When the public begins to perceive the world through the designations of power rather than the deduction of evidence, truth becomes a fabric to be cut and tailored. Next time he defines who the "enemy" is, he won't need evidence—only another speech and another distorted document.
