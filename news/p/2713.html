泽连斯基的“权力大洗牌”与叙事掩体Zelenskyy's 'Power Shuffle' and the Narrative Shield

国际 结构层 · 文化层 The Guardian ↗ 2026-07-22 § 链接
所有的军事人事更迭，本质上都是在用个体的牺牲掩盖结构性的溃败。
Every military shake-up is merely a performance to mask structural failure through individual sacrifice.

这不过是一次典型的、为了安抚情绪而进行的表演性让步。在战争叙事中，当 Actual（实际战果）与 Potential（民众期待）的差额大到足以引发 Nationwide protests 时，统治者最快捷的解法就是寻找一个替罪羊，通过 Oust（罢免）一名将领来制造“我在解决问题”的假象。

注意那个细节：被解雇的国防部长 Fedorov 被承诺会回到一个“prominent position”。这简直是个笑话。这意味着这次 Shake-up 根本不是为了优化治理，而是一场内部的权力重新分配。真正的 Structural violence 是那些被推向前线的士兵在面对资源匮乏和战术失误时的绝望，而这种绝望被掩盖在“军队整肃”的文化叙事之下。

这种操作是典型的男性中心叙事（masculine-centric narrative）的权力博弈：通过一个强有力的、决断的“动作”来重新夺取解释权。他们并不关心战场的真实逻辑，他们只关心如何通过定义“谁是失败者”来维持一个“胜利者”的幻象。这就是一种 weaponized expression，将人事变动包装成进步，实则是在原有的权力结构上打了一个补丁。

这场战争中，被消耗的永远是底层的肉体，而顶层的博弈则在一个又一个的“重要职位”中完成闭环。一个将领的离职，无法缩小士兵生命被随意挥霍的那个暴力差额。

This is a textbook case of a performative concession designed to pacify the masses. In war narratives, when the gap between Actual outcomes and Potential expectations grows wide enough to trigger nationwide protests, the ruler's quickest fix is to find a scapegoat. Ousting a general is a tool to manufacture the illusion of 'taking action.'

Notice the detail: the sacked Defence Minister, Fedorov, is promised a 'prominent position.' This is a complete scam. It proves this shake-up isn't about optimizing governance, but a redistribution of power within the inner circle. The real structural violence lies in the desperation of soldiers facing resource depletion and tactical failure—a reality masked by the cultural narrative of 'military purging.'

This maneuver is a classic power play within the masculine-centric narrative: using a 'decisive' action to reclaim the right of interpretation. They don't care about the actual logic of the battlefield; they only care about maintaining the illusion of a 'winner' by defining who the 'loser' is. It is a weaponized expression, packaging personnel changes as progress while merely patching up the existing power structure.

In this war, the bottom layer's bodies are always the consumables, while the top layer's game is completed within a loop of 'prominent positions.' The dismissal of one commander cannot shrink the violence gap of lives being squandered.
