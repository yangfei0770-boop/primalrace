制造漏洞的艺术：特朗普及他的“选举安全”ScamThe Art of Manufacturing Vulnerabilities: Trump's Election Security Scam

国际 结构层 · 文化层 The New York Times ↗ 2026-07-17 § 链接
通过定义一个不存在的危机来夺取解释权，是典型的武器化叙事。
Defining a non-existent crisis to seize the power of interpretation is a classic weaponization of expression.

特朗普及他的团队正在进行一场教科书级别的“表达武器化”操作。逻辑闭环极其简单：首先通过文化层（cultural layer）密集投放“选举被操纵”的叙事，在公众认知中制造一个名为“漏洞”的幻象；然后，在结构层（structural layer）通过行政权力拆除真实的保护机制，从而人为制造出他口中所谓的“脆弱性”。

这根本不是在修复漏洞，而是在制造漏洞。他通过定义“什么是事实”来夺取解释权，将一个原本稳定的系统描述为“破碎”，目的不是为了构建 confidence，而是为了在未来的博弈中，当结果不符合其预期时，可以立即启动预设的“被窃取”剧本。这种通过摧毁 Actual 来制造 Potential 差额的行为，本质上就是一种结构性暴力。

最讽刺的是，他发布的所谓支持文件在逻辑上完全不支持他的结论。但对于一个掌控了认知入口的人来说，文件内容并不重要，重要的是“发布文件”这个动作本身。这是一种表演性的权力展示，旨在诱导共谋者（complicit followers）忽略事实，只接收他定义的“真实”。

Trump and his team are executing a textbook case of the weaponization of expression. The logic is a simple closed loop: first, deploy a narrative of "rigged elections" at the cultural layer to create a phantom "vulnerability" in public perception; then, at the structural layer, dismantle actual protections to artificially manufacture the very fragility he claims exists.

This isn't about fixing vulnerabilities; it's about manufacturing them. By defining "what is fact," he seizes the power of interpretation, describing a stable system as "broken" so that when future results defy his will, he can immediately trigger the pre-installed "stolen" script. This act of destroying the Actual to create a gap in Potential is, in essence, structural violence.

The irony is that the documents he released logically contradict his conclusions. But for someone who controls the cognitive entry points, the content of the documents is irrelevant; the act of "releasing" them is the performance. It is a display of power designed to lead complicit followers to ignore the evidence and embrace his manufactured reality.
