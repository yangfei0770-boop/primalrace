用换尺子掩盖抢劫：特朗普的贸易叙事骗局The Tariff Scam: Trump's Art of Manufacturing Economic Reality

国际 结构层 · 文化层 · 元暴力 The Guardian ↗ 2026-07-23 § 链接
通过重新定义指标来掩盖结构性掠夺，是典型的元暴力表达。
Redefining metrics to mask structural plunder is a classic manifestation of meta-violence.

特朗普政府再次祭出关税大棒，本质上是一场关于“真实”的制造战争。当最高法院判定之前的关税非法时，这套权力机器并没有反思掠夺的合法性，而是迅速通过一个 10% 的临时方案进行补丁，并在失效前夕准备新一轮的 sweeping tariffs。这种操作逻辑极其简单：只要我掌握了定义规则的权力，非法可以通过“重新定义”变为合法。

最精彩的表演在 Greer 对 Elizabeth Warren 的回答中。面对物价上涨的指控，他直接抛出“核心通胀率 (Core inflation)”这个被操纵的指标。Core inflation 剔除了食品和能源——而这两项恰恰是底层民众生存最基础、受关税波动影响最直接的成本。这就是典型的武器化叙事：通过选择性地定义“什么是事实”，将结构性的生活成本增加，在统计学上抹除为“通胀下降”。

这种“现实不配合就重新定义现实”的机制，与某些科技巨头宣布新定律来掩盖能力缺失如出一辙。它不是在解决经济问题，而是在进行一场存在性博弈：让底层民众在被抢劫的同时，通过一套被精心设计的指标，相信自己正处于“工业复兴”的宏大叙事中。这不仅是经济政策，更是元暴力在公共空间的一次高效投放，旨在让受害者在认知入口被堵死前，先接受一套有利于掠夺者的解释权。

The Trump administration's latest wave of aggressive tariffs is not an economic strategy, but a war over the manufacturing of 'truth.' When the Supreme Court ruled previous tariffs illegal, the machine didn't seek legitimacy; it simply patched the leak with a 10% regime and prepared for the next strike. The logic is simple: if you control the power to define the rules, the 'illegal' can be rebranded as 'necessary' through a simple shift in narrative.

The peak of this performance is Jamieson Greer's exchange with Elizabeth Warren. To deny that tariffs drive up prices, Greer weaponized the concept of 'Core inflation.' By citing a metric that conveniently excludes food and energy—the very essentials where the cost of tariffs hits the poorest most violently—he performed a statistical erasure of suffering. This is the weaponization of expression: choosing a specific cognitive entry point to transform structural violence into a 'positive' trend.

This mechanism of 'redefining reality when reality refuses to cooperate' is the same scam used by tech giants who invent new laws to hide their inability to scale. It is not about revitalizing heartlands; it is an existential game where the powerful seize the right to interpret facts. The goal is to ensure that while the masses are being plundered, they are kept sedated by a masculine-centric narrative of 'industrial rebirth,' effectively closing the cognitive entrance to their own exploitation.
