被献祭的肉身与被掩盖的元暴力Sacrificed Bodies and the Mask of Meta-Violence

国际 直接层 · 结构层 · 元暴力 The New York Times ↗ 2026-07-20 § 链接
战争叙事将人降格为可消耗的工具，而这种消耗本质上是男性中心叙事的自我吞噬。
War narratives degrade humans into consumable tools, a process of self-devouring driven by masculine-centric narratives.

五角大楼公布了死在约旦的两名士兵姓名：一个25岁的中尉，一个19岁的列兵。在主流新闻的叙事里，这被处理成一个关于“牺牲”和“冲突升级”的军事简报。但如果用加尔通的暴力三角来看，这里发生的是最典型的 direct violence，而支撑这种直接暴力的底层逻辑，正是那个名为“国家利益”或“地缘政治”的 meta violence（元暴力）。

战争叙事最阴险的地方在于它制造了一种“英雄化”的认知入口。它把具体的个体——比如那个才19岁的 Isabella Gonzales——从一个拥有主体性的 a human 简化为一个“active-duty soldier”的标签。当个体被降格为可消耗的工具，其存在性就被彻底剥夺了。无论死者是男性还是女性，在战争机器的逻辑中，他们都被客体化为一种“筹码”。

有趣的是，这种暴力结构在男性中心叙事中形成了一个闭环：男性决策者定义宏大叙事，男性士兵在战场上执行，而这种“用人命交换意义”的行为被定义为“真男人”的荣耀。这是一种极端的共谋。即便在死者中出现了女性，她也被纳入这套男性化的军事逻辑中，成为了这台战争机器的零件。这种共谋不仅在军队内部，更在那些消费“牺牲”叙事的公众心中。

17个人死亡，而新闻的结尾是“双方攻击升级”。这种冷漠的数字化处理正是 structural violence 的体现：个体的生命被简化为统计数据，而推动数据增长的动力，依然是那个权力顶端的、由男性垄断的解释权。他们定义谁在进攻，谁在防卫，而底层的肉身则在这些定义之间被撕碎。

The Pentagon released the names of two soldiers killed in Jordan: a 25-year-old Lieutenant and a 19-year-old Private. In mainstream media, this is framed as a military brief on "sacrifice" and "escalation." But through Galtung’s Violence Triangle, we see direct violence fueled by the meta-violence of "national interest" and "geopolitics."

The most insidious part of war narratives is the weaponized entry point of "heroism." It strips a human—like 19-year-old Isabella Gonzales—of her subjectivity, reducing her to a label: "active-duty soldier." When an individual is degraded into a consumable tool, their existence is erased. Whether male or female, they are objectified as mere chips in a high-stakes game.

This violence forms a closed loop within the masculine-centric narrative: men in power define the grand narrative, men on the ground execute it, and the act of "exchanging lives for meaning" is branded as the glory of a "real man." This is a profound complicity. Even the presence of a female soldier doesn't break the cycle; she is simply absorbed into the masculine military logic, becoming another cog in the machine.

Seventeen dead, and the story ends with "spiraling attacks." This cold, numerical processing is the essence of structural violence: individual lives are reduced to statistics, while the engine driving those numbers remains the monopoly of interpretation held by those at the top. They define the aggressor and the defender, while the physical bodies are torn apart between these definitions.
