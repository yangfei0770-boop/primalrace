特朗普威胁袭击所谓的“镐山”（Pickaxe Mountain），这出戏的剧本极其拙劣。根据报道，美国情报部门自己都承认那里“没有任何活动”，甚至专家认为目前的钻地弹也打不透。在这种 Actual 毫无进展的情况下，特朗普依然在公共空间密集投放“重击”这种词汇，这根本不是军事战略，而是一次典型的 Masculine-centric narrative 的自我表演。

战争叙事在这里被彻底武器化。在这种叙事中，核设施被抽象成一个可以被“击碎”的客体，而总统则扮演那个掌控绝对力量的救世主/破坏者。这种表达的入口不是为了解决核扩散问题——因为如果目标是核不扩散，那么在已知对方没有活动的情况下发动攻击纯属自杀——而是为了在认知空间里维持一种“强力”的表型。这种对力量感的病态迷恋，正是元暴力的核心：将人（无论是伊朗平民还是美国士兵）降格为可消耗的工具，用来交换一种名为“霸权”的宏大意义。

这是一场典型的共谋。军工复合体提供昂贵的钻地弹，媒体提供关于“地下堡垒”的神秘叙事，而政治操盘手则利用这种紧张感来掩盖其实际政策的无能。他们共同构建了一个“强人对决”的虚拟剧场，让大众在恐惧与崇拜中忘记了真正的 Structural violence：那些被战争机器撕碎的日常，以及被掩盖在“国家安全”之下的资源掠夺。在这种男本位的博弈中，唯一真实的是死亡，而所有关于“正义”或“威慑”的解释权，都不过是掩盖暴力差额的遮羞布。

Trump's threats to strike 'Pickaxe Mountain' are a masterclass in bad scripting. The reports explicitly state that US intelligence sees 'no activity there' and experts doubt the effectiveness of current bunker busters. When the Actual is zero, yet the rhetoric remains 'heavy,' we are not witnessing military strategy, but a classic performance of Masculine-centric narrative.

War narratives are weaponized here. The nuclear site is abstracted into an object to be 'smashed,' while the President plays the role of the omnipotent destroyer. The entry point of this expression is not nuclear non-proliferation—for if that were the goal, attacking an inactive site would be irrational—but the maintenance of a 'powerful' phenotype in the cognitive space. This pathological obsession with dominance is the essence of meta-violence: degrading humans (both Iranian civilians and US soldiers) into consumable tools to exchange for a grand meaning called 'Hegemony.'

This is a systemic complicity. The military-industrial complex provides expensive munitions, the media constructs the mysterious narrative of 'underground fortresses,' and the political operator uses this tension to mask policy incompetence. Together, they build a virtual theater of 'strongman confrontation,' distracting the public from the Structural violence: the shattered lives and the resource plunder hidden behind 'national security.' In this masculine-centric game, the only truth is death, while all claims of 'justice' or 'deterrence' are merely shams used to cover the gap of violence.
