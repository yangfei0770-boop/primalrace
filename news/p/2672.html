CPI 的数字游戏与被掩盖的结构性剥削The CPI Numbers Game and the Masking of Structural Exploitation

国际 结构层 · 文化层 The Guardian ↗ 2026-07-22 § 链接
宏观数据的下降不等于个体生存差额的缩小，它是政治表演的掩体。
A drop in macro data is not a reduction in individual existential deficit; it is a cover for political performance.

通胀率从 2.8% 跌到 2.6%，在经济学家和政客眼中是“利好”，但在加尔通的暴力三角里，这只是 cultural layer 的一次叙事修辞。CPI 的下降是由石油和运输成本的波动驱动的，这意味着这次所谓的“利好”本质上是地缘政治博弈的副产品，而非结构性的资源重分配。当 Andy Burnham 将此作为其“生活成本计划”的助力时，他实际上是在利用一个随机的外部变量来掩盖 structural violence 的延续：即生活成本的基准线已经处于高位，而 disposable income 的增加依然依赖于资本市场的施舍而非权力结构的转移。

这种典型的“宏观叙事”是元暴力的变体——它用一个平均数掩盖了具体的生存痛点。对于处于结构性弱势的群体，尤其是那些在家庭照料责任中被剥夺劳动参与权的女性，2.6% 的通胀率无法抵消她们在劳动力市场中面临的 Motherhood Penalty。当中央银行在讨论 3.75% 的利率时，他们讨论的是货币工具，而没讨论这些工具如何通过房租和信贷成本，精准地收割那些没有资产支撑的底层人口。

不要被这种“好消息”欺骗。如果 Potential − Actual 的差额没有通过制度性的权力让渡而缩小，那么 CPI 的每一次波动都只是在给这个巨大的剥削机器换一套新的皮肤。这不是胜利，这只是一个暂时的、随机的波动，被包装成了政客的功劳。

Inflation dropping from 2.8% to 2.6% is a "boost" for economists and politicians, but in Galtung's Violence Triangle, this is merely a rhetorical move at the cultural layer. The decline in CPI is driven by fluctuations in petrol and transport costs, meaning this "good news" is a byproduct of geopolitical gambling rather than a structural redistribution of resources. When Andy Burnham frames this as a lift for his "cost of living plans," he is using a random external variable to mask the persistence of structural violence: the baseline cost of living remains high, and the increase in disposable income still depends on the whims of capital markets rather than a shift in power structures.

This kind of "macro-narrative" is a variant of meta-violence—using an average to erase specific existential pain. For structurally disadvantaged groups, particularly women stripped of labor participation due to care responsibilities, a 2.6% inflation rate cannot offset the Motherhood Penalty they face in the job market. While the Bank of England debates a 3.75% interest rate, they are discussing monetary tools, ignoring how these tools, through rent and credit costs, precisely harvest those without asset backing.

Do not be deceived by this "good news." Unless the gap between Potential and Actual is narrowed through a systemic transfer of power, every fluctuation of the CPI is just the exploitation machine changing its skin. This is not a victory; it is a temporary, random fluctuation packaged as political achievement.
