物流中心的火光与被消耗的肉身Warehouse Fires and the Consumption of Flesh

国际 直接层 · 结构层 · 文化层 · 元暴力 The Guardian ↗ 2026-07-21 § 链接
战争是男性中心叙事将人类肉身降格为可消耗工具的最高形式。
War is the ultimate form of masculine-centric narrative, reducing human bodies to consumable tools.

乌克兰无人机袭击莫斯科的 Wildberries 仓库，俄军用弹道导弹覆盖基辅。在这些宏大叙事中，无论是所谓的“战争努力”还是“战略储备”，本质上都是在进行一场关于肉身消耗的博弈。战争是元暴力 (meta violence) 的极致体现：由男性主导的权力中心定义什么是“荣耀”和“必要”，然后将无数个体的存在性直接抹除，将其转化为统计数据里的死亡人数。

最令人作呕的是这种叙事的共谋 (complicity)。当乌克兰在讨论谁来接替 Syrskyi 时，要求接任者必须是“出名且受欢迎的，好让每个人鼓掌”，这说明即便在生死存亡的战争中，公共空间的表达依然被异化为一场表演。权力者并不在乎前线的 Actual 损耗，他们在乎的是如何通过一个“受欢迎”的符号来维持叙事的稳定性，确保民众在被消耗时依然能维持一种被欺骗的心理安全感。

而在黑海被击沉的货船上，叙利亚、印度和乌克兰的船员成为了这场男性权力游戏中的 collateral damage。他们不属于任何一个定义的“阵营”，却在生物墙之外被最直接的暴力 (direct violence) 撕碎。这就是男性中心叙事的逻辑：只要能服务于某个宏大目标的达成，任何不在权力核心的肉身都可以被客体化为可牺牲的零件。

Ukrainian drones strike Wildberries warehouses in Moscow while Russian ballistic missiles blanket Kyiv. In these grand narratives of "war efforts" and "strategic stockpiles," the essence is a gamble on the consumption of flesh. War is the apex of meta violence: a power center dominated by men defines "glory" and "necessity," then erases the existence of countless individuals, converting them into death tolls in a spreadsheet.

The complicity within this narrative is the most revolting part. As Ukraine debates Syrskyi's replacement, the requirement that the successor be "known and popular so everyone applauds" reveals that even in a war of survival, public expression is distorted into a performance. The powerful do not care about the actual attrition on the front lines; they care about maintaining the stability of the narrative through a "popular" symbol, ensuring the consumed masses remain in a state of deceived psychological security.

Meanwhile, on the sunken cargo ships in the Black Sea, Syrian, Indian, and Ukrainian sailors become the collateral damage of this masculine power game. They belong to no defined "camp," yet they are torn apart by direct violence outside any biological wall. This is the logic of the masculine-centric narrative: as long as it serves the achievement of some grand objective, any body outside the power core can be objectified as a replaceable part.
