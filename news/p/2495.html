CPAC GB：一场关于“存在性”的右翼自救与叙事诈骗CPAC GB: A Right-Wing Salvage Operation and Narrative Scam

国际 文化层 · 元暴力 The Guardian ↗ 2026-07-17 § 链接
用“被取代”的虚构恐惧，掩盖权力失效后的主体性危机。
Manufacturing a fictional fear of 'replacement' to mask the existential crisis of failing power.

Liz Truss 搞这个 CPAC GB，本质上是一场典型的“表达武器化”尝试。一个在权力中心被迅速抛弃的政治生物，试图通过在文化层制造一种“被深层政府（deep state）迫害”的受害者叙事，来重新夺回她的解释权。这不仅是政治重启，而是一次拙劣的认知入口抢占。

最令人作呕的是这场会议对“取代” (Replacement) 叙事的消费。当 George Simion 谈论“大替换”和“回迁”时，他们实际上是在制造一种伪造的 structural violence，试图说服白人底层：你们的 Potential 被少数族裔抢走了。这是一种典型的 scam：通过定义一个虚构的敌人，将真实的阶级剥削转化为种族焦虑。Braverman 更是将这种逻辑推向极致，声称“追求平等”反而导致了“反白人种族主义”。这在逻辑上就是一种元暴力的反向操作——把既得利益者的特权定义为“被剥夺的权利”。

这场集会是右翼共谋者的一个绝佳切片。从 Bitcoin 的资本操盘到极右翼的口号，他们通过一套“常识政治” (common sense politics) 的包装，试图建立一个排他性的阵营。在这个阵营里，所谓的“自由”和“主权”不过是武器化的标签，用来筛选掉任何不符合他们利益的异己。而 Truss 所谓的“激励右翼” (galvanising the right)，其实就是想在一个由她定义的新规则中，重新获得作为“主体”的存在感。

至于那些支付高达 10,000 英镑入场费的参会者，他们买的不是门票，而是一种“被选中的”身份认同。他们通过共谋这种极端叙事，在心理上完成了从“被时代抛弃的失败者”到“反抗深层政府的战士”的身份转换。这正是存在性战争中最阴暗的环节：当一个人在真实的博弈中输掉主体性时，最快的方式就是扮演一个被定义的角色。

Liz Truss’s orchestration of CPAC GB is a textbook case of the weaponisation of expression. A political entity swiftly discarded by the power center is attempting to reclaim its interpretative authority by manufacturing a cultural narrative of being persecuted by the 'deep state.' This is not a political reboot; it is a clumsy attempt to hijack the cognitive entry points of the public.

The most repulsive element is the consumption of the 'Replacement' narrative. When George Simion speaks of the 'Great Replacement' and 'remigration,' he is fabricating a structural violence to convince the white working class that their Potential has been stolen by ethnic minorities. This is a classic scam: converting real class exploitation into racial anxiety by defining a phantom enemy. Braverman pushes this to the extreme, claiming that 'attempts to create equality' have institutionalised 'anti-white racism.' This is a perverse inversion of meta-violence—defining the privileges of the dominant class as 'stolen rights.'

This event is a perfect slice of the co-conspirators' logic within the right wing. From the capital manipulation of Bitcoin to far-right slogans, they use the packaging of 'common sense politics' to build an exclusionary camp. In this space, 'liberty' and 'sovereignty' are merely weaponised labels used to filter out any dissidents. Truss’s goal of 'galvanising the right' is simply an attempt to regain her existence as a 'subject' within a new set of rules she defines.

As for the attendees paying up to £10,000, they aren't buying tickets; they are buying an identity. By conspiring in this extreme narrative, they achieve a psychological transition from 'failures discarded by the era' to 'warriors fighting the deep state.' This is the darkest part of the existential war: when an individual loses their subjectivity in a real game, the fastest escape is to perform a role assigned by a master narrator.
