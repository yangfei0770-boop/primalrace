用医学词汇给暴力洗白，是最高级的共谋Medicalizing Violence: The Ultimate Act of Complicity

国际 文化层 · 结构层 · 元暴力 The Guardian ↗ 2026-07-22 § 链接
将异见者定义为“癌症”，是将结构暴力转化为合法屠杀的认知入口。
Defining dissent as 'cancer' is the cognitive entry point for transforming structural violence into legitimate slaughter.

这篇文章最惊悚的不是在描述民主的死亡，而是在揭露一种极其典型的表达武器化：medicalization of killing。当白宫副幕僚长把左翼政治定义为“文明的致命癌症”，并宣称抗议者的外貌、装扮是“内在仇恨的体现”时，他完成了一次极其阴险的认知入口夺取。他不再讨论政见，而是在讨论“生物学缺陷”和“病理状态”。

这正是典型的文化暴力（cultural violence）。通过将对方定义为“肿瘤”或“病菌”，施暴者直接跳过了法律和伦理的博弈，将“清除”包装成“治疗”。在这种叙事下，逮捕、没收财产、甚至肉体消灭，都变成了为了拯救“国家这个病人”而必须采取的医疗手段。这种表达方式最恶心的地方在于，它利用了人们对医学权威的本能信任，把元暴力（meta violence）伪装成科学的客观性。

更令人绝望的是共谋者的反应。主流媒体和事实核查员在忙着解析总统关于选举数据的谎言（那是最响亮的噪音），却忽略了早晨那场关于“定义谁是癌症”的权力交接。这种 triage error（分诊错误）本质上是对结构性暴力的钝感。当一个群体被定义为“非人”或“病原体”时，他们就失去了在社会博弈中获得“公正表达”的所有筹码。因为在医生面前，肿瘤是没有表达权的，它只有被切除的命运。

这场存在性战争的胜负不在于选票的计数，而在于谁掌握了定义“正常人”的权力。一旦“正常”变成了筛选敌我的生物墙，那么任何不符合权力审美的人，都将成为被合法清除的“病灶”。

The horror of this piece isn't just the death of democracy, but the exposure of a sinister weaponization of expression: the medicalization of killing. When the White House deputy chief of staff labels leftist politics a 'fatal cancer of civilization' and claims protesters' appearances are 'manifestations of inner hatred,' he is seizing a cognitive entry point. He is no longer debating policy; he is diagnosing biological defects and pathological states.

This is a textbook example of cultural violence. By defining the enemy as a 'tumor' or 'bacillus,' the aggressor bypasses legal and ethical gambling, rebranding 'extermination' as 'cure.' In this narrative, arrests, defunding, and physical elimination are framed as necessary medical interventions to save the 'patient'—the nation. The most perverse part is how it leverages the instinctive trust in medical authority to disguise meta violence as scientific objectivity.

Even more desperate is the behavior of the complicitors. Mainstream media and fact-checkers are obsessed with parsing the president's lies about voter files—the loudest noise—while ignoring the morning's quiet shift in the power to define 'cancer.' This triage error is essentially a numbness to structural violence. Once a group is defined as 'non-human' or 'pathogenic,' they lose all leverage for Just Expressions in the social game. After all, a tumor has no right to speak in front of a surgeon; it only has the fate of being excised.

The outcome of this existential war isn't about the vote count, but about who controls the definition of 'normal.' Once 'normalcy' becomes a biological wall for screening enemies, anyone who fails the power-defined aesthetic becomes a 'lesion' to be legally erased.
