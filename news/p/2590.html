芝加哥检察官的“专业危机”：共谋者的集体离职The Chicago Prosecutor Crisis: A Collapse of Complicity

国际 结构层 · 文化层 The New York Times ↗ 2026-07-20 § 链接
当制度性暴力需要具体的人去按下快门，良心成了共谋成本的度量衡。
Conscience is merely the measure of complicity costs when systemic violence requires a human finger on the trigger.

芝加哥联邦检察官办公室的集体出走，表面上是一场关于“声誉”的专业危机，本质上是 structural violence 在执行层面的崩塌。Trump 政府的 Operation Midway Blitz 试图通过大规模逮捕抗议者和移民来制造一种“秩序”的假象，但这种叙事需要具体的人在法庭上将其转化为法律事实。当检察官们被要求起诉那些注定失败的案件时，他们意识到自己不再是法律的维护者，而成了暴力机器的零件。

这是一场典型的共谋博弈。在男性中心叙事（masculine-centric narrative）构建的权力结构中，这种“扫荡”行动被包装成保护国家安全的强力表达。然而，当这种表达要求执行者通过牺牲专业底线来换取政治效忠时，共谋的成本超过了收益。那些资深检察官的离开，实际上是在用“离职”这种表达方式，否决一个将法律武器化的系统。

但不要 naive 地庆祝这种“正义感”。这种危机的核心不在于几个检察官的良心发现，而在于权力运作的粗糙——当行政指令强行覆盖司法逻辑，导致案件“注定失败”时，共谋者们才意识到这个 scam 已经无法在专业领域内闭环。这并非是对移民或抗议者的救赎，而是一次关于“如何高效地实施暴力”的内部权力冲突。

The mass exodus from the U.S. attorney’s office in Chicago is framed as a 'reputational crisis,' but it is actually a collapse of structural violence at the execution level. The Trump administration's Operation Midway Blitz attempted to manufacture a narrative of 'order' through the mass arrest of immigrants and protesters. However, this weaponized expression required specific individuals to translate political will into legal facts in court.

This is a classic game of complicity. Within the masculine-centric narrative of power, such 'sweeps' are packaged as strong expressions of national security. But when the system demands that prosecutors sacrifice professional integrity for political loyalty, the cost of complicity outweighs the benefit. The departure of these veteran prosecutors is an act of expression—a refusal to be a mere cog in a violence machine.

Do not be naive enough to celebrate this as a victory for 'conscience.' The core of this crisis is not a sudden moral awakening, but the clumsiness of power. When administrative orders override judicial logic to the point that cases are 'doomed to fail,' the co-conspirators realize the scam can no longer close its loop. This isn't a rescue mission for immigrants; it is an internal power struggle over how to implement violence more efficiently.
