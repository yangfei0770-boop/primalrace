用“法律”掩盖的共谋，是元暴力最卑劣的闭环Collusion Masked as Law: The Vile Loop of Meta-Violence

国际 结构层 · 文化层 · 元暴力 The Guardian ↗ 2026-07-23 § 链接
法律在此时不是正义的尺度，而是保护权力共谋者的防御墙。
Law here is not a scale of justice, but a defensive wall for the powerful complicity.

这场针对人权律师 Rajiv Menon 的起诉，本质上是一次典型的 structural violence。当法庭以“藐视法庭”为由，试图惩罚一个在结案陈词中揭露权力勾结的律师时，它在传递一个极其明确的信号：法律的边界，就是权力共谋者的安全区。

Menon 被指控称 Elbit Systems 在“权力走廊中被款待”，这触碰了谁的利益？在元暴力的逻辑里，所谓的“司法公正”和“程序正义”经常被用作武器化的叙事，用来掩盖一个事实——法律体系本身就是由既得利益者共谋而成的。当律师试图在法庭上将“被保护的军工企业”这一事实摆在陪审团面前时，他实际上是在挑战这套共谋机制的解释权。

这种起诉产生了一种极强的 chilling effect（寒蝉效应）。当律师们开始在没有指令的情况下自我审查、修改陈词，这意味着他们被迫进入了一种“假.最优解表达”：通过扮演一个顺从的法律技术员来换取职业生存，代价是主体性的死亡和对真相的背叛。这正是元暴力的精髓——它不需要直接地禁止你说话，它只需要让你在说话之前，先在内心完成一次自我阉割。

人权即女权，因为两者面对的是同样的逻辑：一个由强势者定义事实、掌控解释权、并将其伪装成“文明”与“秩序”的暴力机器。这次对 Menon 的攻击，不过是这台机器在感受到威胁时的一次惯性抽搐。

The prosecution of human rights barrister Rajiv Menon is a textbook case of structural violence. When the court uses 'contempt' to punish a lawyer for exposing the nexus of power in a closing speech, it sends a clear signal: the boundary of the law is the safety zone of the powerful complicity.

Menon was accused of suggesting Elbit Systems was 'wined and dined in the corridors of power.' Whose interests did this touch? In the logic of meta-violence, 'judicial impartiality' and 'procedural justice' are often weaponized narratives used to mask a simple fact—the legal system itself is a product of complicity among the elite. By attempting to bring the reality of a 'protected arms company' before the jury, Menon challenged the monopoly of interpretation held by this system.

This prosecution creates a profound chilling effect. When barristers begin to self-censor and alter their speeches without orders, they are forced into a 'fake optimal expression': playing the role of a compliant legal technician to ensure professional survival, at the cost of their subjectivity and the truth. This is the essence of meta-violence—it doesn't need to forbid speech directly; it simply requires you to perform a self-castration in your mind before you even speak.

Human rights are women's rights, because both confront the same logic: a machine of violence that defines facts, monopolizes interpretation, and disguises it all as 'civilization' and 'order.' This assault on Menon is merely an instinctive spasm of that machine when it feels its grip slipping.
