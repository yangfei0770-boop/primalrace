Google 的 AI 围栏：从“入口”到“终点”的权力闭环Google's AI Fence: The Power Loop from 'Gateway' to 'Destination'

科技 结构层 · 文化层 · 元暴力 The New York Times ↗ 2026-07-21 § 链接
将链接替换为答案，本质是夺取解释权并切断信息的生物多样性。
Replacing links with answers is a seizure of interpretative power and a slaughter of informational biodiversity.

Google 正在完成一次极其阴险的权力跃迁。早期的 Google 扮演的是 Gateway（入口），它通过提供 Hyperlinks 将用户推向 Open Web。在那套叙事里，Google 像是一个公正的图书管理员，它不定义事实，只告诉你事实可能在哪个房间里。但这其实是它的初级阶段——通过控制“通往事实的路径”来获取流量定价权。

现在，AI Mode 正在把这个路径直接掐断。当 Gemini 用一段对话直接替代搜索结果时，Google 从一个“索引者”变成了“定义者”。这不再是简单的技术升级，而是一次典型的 Weaponization of Expression（表达武器化）。它通过制造一个 AI 围栏，将用户禁锢在自己的认知入口内。当用户不再点击链接，Open Web 上的独立出版者、研究者和创作者就失去了存在性的可见度。这意味着，Google 正在垄断“什么是事实”的制造权。

这是一种极其严重的 Structural Violence（结构暴力）。它通过改变信息的分配机制，让无数依赖 Open Web 生存的个体在数字空间中被事实上地“抹除”。更讽刺的是，这种剥夺被包装在“效率”和“便捷”的文化外壳下。当一个公司能够决定你看到的答案，而不再让你接触到产生答案的源头时，它就成了这个数字世界的元暴力执行者——它定义了现实，而你失去了质疑现实的参照系。

Google is executing a sinister power transition. In its early days, Google acted as a Gateway, pushing users toward the Open Web via hyperlinks. In that narrative, Google played the role of an impartial librarian—not defining facts, but directing you to the room where facts resided. This was its primitive stage: gaining pricing power by controlling the 'path to truth.'

Now, AI Mode is severing that path. When Gemini replaces search results with a conversational response, Google evolves from an 'indexer' to a 'definer.' This is not a mere tech upgrade; it is a classic Weaponisation of Expression. By building an AI fence, Google traps users within its own cognitive entry point. As users stop clicking links, independent publishers and creators on the Open Web lose their existential visibility. Google is now monopolizing the manufacturing right of 'what is fact.'

This is profound Structural Violence. By altering the mechanism of information distribution, Google effectively 'erases' countless individuals who rely on the Open Web. The irony is that this dispossession is wrapped in the cultural veneer of 'efficiency' and 'convenience.' When a single corporation decides the answer without letting you see the source, it becomes the executor of meta-violence in the digital realm—it defines reality, and you lose the reference point to challenge it.
