合伙抢羊与合法谋杀：西岸的共谋剧本Partnered Looting and Legalized Murder: The West Bank Complicity Script

国际 直接层 · 结构层 · 文化层 · 元暴力 The Guardian ↗ 2026-07-20 § 链接
当军队成为抢劫者的保镖，暴力就成了制度化的资源掠夺。
When the army becomes the bodyguard for looters, violence becomes institutionalized resource plunder.

这不再是所谓的“冲突”，而是一场极其低劣的合伙抢劫。定居者负责冲在前面抢羊，军队负责在后面开枪。这种 partnership 的本质是 structural violence 的极致外显：通过将抢劫行为包装成“寻找失窃牲畜”，将谋杀包装成“应对袭击”，把原本应由法律维护的秩序直接转化为掠夺的工具。

在这里，加尔通的暴力三角闭环了。直接层是胸口中弹的村民；结构层是军队与定居者共享的水利设施和协同作战的 raid 机制；而文化层则是 IDF 那套惯用的叙事 scam——只要对方扔了一块石头，无论对方是在保护财产还是在绝望反抗，就可以被定义为“恐怖分子”并被合法地射杀。这种叙事为直接暴力提供了完美的掩体，让谋杀看起来像是在执行公务。

最令人作呕的是这种 impunity（免责）的制度化。一个十年没有起诉过杀害平民公民的司法系统，本身就是元暴力的一个节点。它在向所有共谋者发送信号：只要你是这个阵营的，你的暴力就是“正当的”，而对方的死亡只是“必要的代价”。

所谓的“安全检查站”和“临时管制”，其实是武器化表达在物理空间的延伸。它们通过切断流动性，将村庄变成一个无法逃离的猎场。当生活被定义为“不可居住” (unlivable) 时，这已经不是简单的领土争端，而是一场彻头彻尾的 ethnic cleansing 蓝图。在这个剧本里，羊只是借口，目标是让对方在绝望中消失。

This is no longer a 'conflict'; it is a crude partnership in robbery. Settlers lead the charge to steal livestock, while the army follows to provide cover fire. The essence of this partnership is the ultimate manifestation of structural violence: packaging theft as 'searching for stolen livestock' and murder as 'responding to attacks,' transforming the order that law should maintain into a tool for plunder.

Here, Galtung's Violence Triangle closes. The direct layer is the villager shot through the chest; the structural layer is the shared water infrastructure and coordinated raid mechanisms between settlers and the military; and the cultural layer is the IDF's habitual narrative scam—as long as a stone is thrown, regardless of whether the victim is protecting property or resisting in despair, they are labeled 'terrorists' and legally executed. This narrative provides a perfect shield for direct violence, making murder look like official duty.

Most sickening is the institutionalization of impunity. A judicial system that hasn't prosecuted its citizens for killing Palestinian civilians in a decade is a critical node of meta-violence. It signals to all complicity partners: if you belong to this camp, your violence is 'justified,' and the other's death is merely a 'necessary cost.'

Those 'security checkpoints' and 'temporary restrictions' are simply the physical extension of weaponized expression. By cutting off mobility, they turn villages into inescapable hunting grounds. When life is defined as 'unlivable,' this is no longer a mere territorial dispute, but a full-scale blueprint for ethnic cleansing. In this script, the sheep are just an excuse; the goal is to make the other disappear in despair.
