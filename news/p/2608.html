被AI和更年期掩盖的结构性弃子Structural Outcasts Masked by AI and Menopause

哲学 结构层 · 文化层 · 元暴力 The Guardian ↗ 2026-07-21 § 链接
失业焦虑不是个人失败，而是结构暴力在不同生命周期的精准收割。
Job anxiety is not a personal failure, but a precise harvest of structural violence across life cycles.

这篇文章揭露了一个极其残酷的现实：在当前的就业市场中，无论是被AI取代的职场新人，还是陷入“三明治年限”的中年女性，都在经历一场关于存在性的战争。人们习惯于将失业解释为“个人能力不足”或“更年期激素影响”，这正是典型的 cultural violence —— 用个体的生物性或心理缺陷，来掩盖结构性的资源剥夺。

对于50-64岁的英国女性，30%的经济非活跃率被简单地归类为 perimenopause story。这简直是个 scam。将女性的职业中断归因于激素，实际上是在通过生物墙（Biological Wall）将她们客体化，从而合法化她们在劳动力市场中的被抛弃状态。真正的暴力在于 structural 层：当社会要求女性在承担育儿与养老的双重无偿劳动时，没有任何制度在为这种“照顾责任”买单，结果就是女性在公共空间被强制性抹除。

而另一端的年轻人则在面对 AI 制造的认知入口。雇主们迷信 AI 的效率，用算法在毫秒间完成拒绝，这不仅是技术升级，而是一次对人类主体性的大规模屠杀。当 entry-level 岗位消失，整个职业晋升的 pipeline 被切断，这种 scarcity mindset（匮乏心态）让幸存者陷入恐怖的内卷，成为维护这个病态系统的共谋者。

失业的 omertà（沉默契约）正是元暴力的体现。当人们不敢分享失败，只能在私域中消化焦虑时，这种结构性暴力就变得不可见。打破沉默不是为了寻求安慰，而是为了意识到：这不是“你的问题”，而是一个由男性中心叙事主导、由资本与算法共谋的系统性掠夺。

This piece exposes a brutal reality: whether it's entry-level workers replaced by AI or middle-aged women trapped in the "sandwich years," both are fighting a losing existential war. The habit of explaining unemployment as "personal failing" or "hormonal shifts" is a classic form of cultural violence — using biological or psychological defects to mask structural dispossession.

For UK women aged 50-64, a 30% economic inactivity rate is lazily categorized as a perimenopause story. This is a complete scam. Attributing professional hiatus to hormones is a way of objectifying women through the Biological Wall, legitimizing their erasure from the labor market. The real violence is structural: when society demands women perform the double burden of childcare and eldercare without any systemic compensation, they are forcibly deleted from the public sphere.

At the other end, youth are facing the weaponized cognitive entrance of AI. Employers, blinded by a faith in algorithmic efficiency, reject candidates in milliseconds. This isn't just a tech upgrade; it's a mass slaughter of human agency. As entry-level positions vanish and the professional pipeline is severed, the resulting scarcity mindset forces survivors into a terrified complicity, maintaining a pathological system.

The omertà of unemployment is a manifestation of meta-violence. When the unemployed dare not share their failure and must digest anxiety in private, structural violence remains invisible. Breaking the silence is not about seeking comfort, but about realizing that this is not a "you problem" — it is a systemic plunder orchestrated by a masculine-centric narrative and the complicity of capital and algorithms.
