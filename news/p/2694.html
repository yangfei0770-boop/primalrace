隔离区里的“生物墙”与权力快感The 'Biological Wall' of Quarantine and the Pleasure of Power

国际 结构层 · 文化层 · 元暴力 The New York Times ↗ 2026-07-22 § 链接
隔离不是为了防疫，而是通过剥夺表达空间来确认统治者的存在性。
Quarantine is not about prevention, but about confirming the ruler's existence by stripping away the subject's expression.

这起汉坦病毒隔离事件，本质上是一次典型的 structural violence。当 CDC 的科学家已经建议居家监测，而官方依然选择将 18 个美国公民关进 Omaha 的隔离设施时，这已经脱离了医学逻辑，进入了权力博弈的领域。隔离区在这里成了一堵人为制造的“生物墙”，它通过物理切断个体的表达空间，将活生生的人降格为待处理的“样本”。

最令人作呕的是那个视频通话中的细节：一个追求自然与自由的旅行者在哀求，而官员 Dr. Fitter 的回应是“我将把这个问题递交上去”。这种典型的 bureaucratic complicity（官僚共谋），通过将责任推向一个不可见的、更高层的“系统”，完成了对个体主体性的彻底抹杀。在这种叙事里，个体的痛苦被定义为“为了公共安全”的必要代价，而这种“安全”实际上成了统治者通过掌控他人身体来获得权力快感的 weaponized narrative。

这和早期的 Covid 隔离如出一辙：当 Potential（本可达到的自由与理性防疫）与 Actual（被囚禁在绝望中的 42 天）之间出现巨大差额时，这个差额就是暴力。官方通过垄断解释权，将一个医学上的 incubation period（潜伏期）转化为一场关于服从的压力测试。在这种元暴力的运作下，所谓的“科学防疫”仅仅是掩盖权力傲慢的文化外壳。

The Hantavirus quarantine ordeal is a textbook case of structural violence. When CDC scientists recommended home monitoring, yet officials insisted on confining 18 Americans in the Omaha facility, the logic shifted from medicine to power dynamics. The quarantine zone became an artificial 'biological wall,' physically severing an individual's expression and degrading human beings into 'samples' to be processed.

Most repulsive is the detail of the video call: a traveler, driven by a love for nature and freedom, pleads for reason, only to be met with Dr. Fitter's response that he would 'pass this up.' This is pure bureaucratic complicity. By pushing responsibility toward an invisible, higher-level 'system,' the official completes the erasure of the individual's subjectivity. In this narrative, personal suffering is rebranded as a 'necessary price for public safety'—a weaponized narrative where 'safety' is merely a pretext for the ruler's pleasure in controlling bodies.

This echoes the early days of Covid: when a massive gap opens between the Potential (rational prevention and freedom) and the Actual (42 days of imprisonment), that gap is violence. By monopolizing the power of interpretation, the authorities transformed a medical incubation period into a stress test for obedience. Under the influence of meta-violence, so-called 'scientific prevention' is nothing more than a cultural facade masking institutional arrogance.
