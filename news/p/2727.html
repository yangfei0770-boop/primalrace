战争是男性中心叙事将人客体化为消耗品的终极表达。
War is the ultimate expression of masculine-centric narrative, objectifying humans as consumable resources.

这篇新闻的叙事重心在 $95 的油价、Goldman Sachs 的预测以及 IEA 的“缓冲因素”上。这是一种典型的武器化表达：用经济指标的波动来掩盖直接暴力的血腥，将一场关乎生死的战争转化为一场关于成本、供应和利润的会计审计。当 Equinor 宣布利润翻倍时，战争在文化层被成功地编码成了“商业机会”。

这就是元暴力的运作方式。这场冲突的本质是男性统治者在公共空间进行的博弈，他们通过定义“国防学说”和“国家安全”来夺取解释权，而代价则是被客体化为战争工具的士兵，以及被当作筹码袭击的平民基础设施。在这种 masculine-centric narrative 中，不管是 Trump 的威胁还是 Araghchi 的“以眼还眼”，都是在进行一场关于权力席位的存在性战争，而具体的人——无论性别——都成了这场博弈中可被消耗的资源。

最讽刺的是，这种暴力被包装在“国际法”和“外交努力”的文明掩体之下。实际上，当双方开始通过攻击淡水厂和电站来获取 leverage 时，这已经撕开了文明的伪装。这不仅是结构性暴力的升级，更是直接暴力的狂欢。在这种逻辑里，人权被排在了油价曲线之后，而这种将人降格为手段的逻辑，正是所有性别暴力和种族暴力的原初蓝图。

This news focuses on the $95 oil mark, Goldman Sachs' forecasts, and the IEA's "cushioning factors." This is a textbook example of weaponized expression: using economic fluctuations to mask the gore of direct violence, transforming a life-and-death war into an accounting audit of costs, supplies, and profits. When Equinor announces doubled profits, war is successfully encoded as a "business opportunity" at the cultural layer.

This is how meta-violence operates. The essence of this conflict is a game played by male rulers in the public sphere. By defining "defense doctrines" and "national security," they seize the power of interpretation. The cost is the soldiers objectified as tools and the civilian infrastructure used as bargaining chips. In this masculine-centric narrative, whether it is Trump's threats or Araghchi's "eye for an eye," it is an existential war over power seats, where individual humans are reduced to consumable resources.

The irony lies in how this violence is sheltered under the guise of "international law" and "diplomatic efforts." In reality, when both sides seek leverage by attacking desalination plants and power grids, the mask of civilization is torn away. This is not just an escalation of structural violence, but a carnival of direct violence. In this logic, human rights are ranked below oil price curves—a logic of degrading humans into means, which serves as the original blueprint for all gender and racial violence.
