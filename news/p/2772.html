浪漫爱陷阱里的“价值”计算题The 'Value' Calculus in the Romantic Love Trap

性别 文化层 The Guardian ↗ 2026-07-23 § 链接
将个体价值锚定在他人的选择上，是典型的浪漫爱叙事 scam。
Anchoring individual value to another's choice is a classic romantic love narrative scam.

这个男性的痛苦在于他把一段八个月的恋爱当成了对自己人生价值的“定价单”。在他看来，一个“优秀且宽容”的女性决定离开他，就等同于他在客观价值市场上被判定为“不合格”。这种逻辑极其典型：他不是在经历失去，而是在进行一场关于存在性的亏损核算。这就是浪漫爱叙事最阴险的地方——它诱导个体将主体性让渡给对方，把对方的认可当作唯一的生存证明。

周围人的反应更是一种共谋。那些说“你应该更有信心”的人，潜意识里认为一段关系的建立是对男性某种“能力”的背书，而关系的结束则是这种能力的丧失。在这种 masculine-centric narrative 中，恋爱被异化成了某种勋章或等级证明，而非两个独立人格的碰撞。他感到的“不被接纳”，本质上是他内化了这套评价体系，在没有观众的时刻，依然在用他者的尺子对自己进行 structural violence。

专栏作家的建议试图通过“适配度”来消解这种绝望，这在心理抚慰层面是有效的，但在结构分析上依然温和。真正的破局不在于寻找下一个“适配”的人，而在于意识到：任何基于“被选择”而获得的自尊，都是建立在沙滩上的。只要你还需要通过另一个人的“认可”来确证自己的 worth，你就在这场存在性战争中处于绝对劣势。因为你的定价权始终在对方手里。

This man's suffering stems from treating an eight-month relationship as a 'price tag' for his life's value. In his mind, the decision of a 'wonderful and understanding' woman to leave is equivalent to being judged as 'substandard' in an objective value market. This logic is textbook: he isn't just experiencing loss, but conducting a loss-calculation of his own existence. This is the most insidious part of the romantic love narrative—it lures individuals into surrendering their subjectivity to another, treating the other's approval as the sole proof of existence.

The reactions of people around him are a form of complicity. Those saying "you should feel more confident" subconsciously believe that entering a relationship is a validation of a man's 'capability,' and ending it is a loss of that status. In this masculine-centric narrative, romance is alienated into a medal or a rank, rather than a collision of two independent personalities. His feeling of 'not being accepted' is essentially the internalization of this evaluation system; even in the absence of an audience, he continues to apply the other's ruler to himself, practicing a form of structural violence.

The columnist's advice attempts to dissolve this despair through the concept of 'fit,' which is effective for psychological comfort but too soft for structural analysis. The real breakthrough isn't finding the next 'fit' person, but realizing that any self-esteem based on 'being chosen' is built on sand. As long as you require another's 'approval' to confirm your worth, you remain at a total disadvantage in this existential war. Because your pricing power always remains in the hands of the other.
