当技术将“凝视”隐形化，它就成了高效的暴力武器。
When technology makes the 'gaze' invisible, it becomes a high-efficiency weapon of violence.

Meta 的 VP 用一种极其典型的 masculine-centric 叙事来推销他的智能眼镜：一个慈父，在才艺比赛中“抬头挺胸”地记录女儿。这种“生活在当下”的温情叙事是一个巨大的 scam。它通过定义一个 wholesome 的使用场景，试图掩盖该产品在结构上为 covert filming（隐秘拍摄）提供的完美入口。

在暴力三角中，这属于典型的 cultural violence 为 structural violence 铺路。Meta 把“不漏掉任何瞬间”包装成对父母的赋权，但实际上，它在物理层面消解了被拍摄者的防御可能。那个微小的闪光灯在现实博弈中毫无意义，它只是为了在 PR 层面声明自己“尽到了告知义务”，而实际上，它在鼓励一种新的、无需手机屏幕遮挡的数字化狩猎。

更阴暗的共谋在于，这些图像在 AI 时代变成了生成 CSAM（儿童性虐待材料）的 raw material。这不再仅仅是隐私泄露，而是一种对原初种族——尤其是最弱势的儿童——的生物性掠夺。Meta 试图将责任推给“个体不应主动滥用”，这是典型的逃避机制。它在制造一个能让 creep 们 anons 且高效的工具，然后对着受害者说：请通过法律手段去制约那些坏人。

所谓的“盲人辅助”叙事则是典型的武器化伪装。用极少数的 humanitarian case 来为大众市场的掠夺性产品提供道德豁免权，这种叙事陷阱让监管者在“文明”与“效率”之间犹豫。但事实是，一个被设计成“不让人感到尴尬”的记录设备，本质上就是为了在不被察觉的情况下侵占他人的存在性。

Meta's VP markets his smartglasses using a textbook masculine-centric narrative: a doting father 'living in the moment' while filming his daughter. This wholesome narrative is a total scam. By defining a sentimental use-case, it attempts to mask the structural entry point the product provides for covert filming.

In the Violence Triangle, this is cultural violence paving the way for structural violence. Meta packages 'never miss a moment' as empowerment for parents, while physically erasing the possibility of defense for the observed. That tiny flashing light is meaningless in a real-world game; it exists only as a PR shield to claim 'due notice,' while actually enabling a new form of digital hunting that no longer requires the visible barrier of a phone screen.

The darker complicity lies in how these images become raw material for AI-generated CSAM. This is no longer just a privacy breach; it is a biological predation of the Primal Race, specifically the most vulnerable children. Meta's argument that individuals should not 'actively exploit' the tech is a classic evasion mechanism. They are building a tool for creeps to be anonymous and efficient, then telling victims: 'Please rely on the law to stop the bad guys.'

The 'blind assistance' narrative is a weaponized camouflage. Using a few humanitarian cases to provide moral immunity for a predatory mass-market product is a narrative trap that makes regulators hesitate between 'civilization' and 'efficiency.' The truth is, a recording device designed to be 'not embarrassing' is fundamentally designed to occupy another's existence without their knowledge.
