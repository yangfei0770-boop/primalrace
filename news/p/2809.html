国际象棋的权力洗牌与叙事快餐The Power Shuffle of World Chess and Narrative Fast Food

国际 结构层 · 文化层 · 元暴力 The Guardian ↗ 2026-07-24 § 链接
制裁是结构暴力的显性化，而网红转向是廉价的叙事表演。
Sanctions are the manifestation of structural violence; influencer pivots are cheap narrative performances.

国际棋联（FIDE）主席因为被制裁而下台，这件事本身就是个绝佳的讽刺。在男权中心叙事（masculine-centric narrative）中，国际象棋被塑造为纯粹的理智与战略博弈，但现实是，这个领域的解释权始终被像德沃科维奇这样身兼政府高职的男性垄断。当结构层（structural layer）的政治制裁切断了资金与身份的连接，所谓的“纯粹理智”瞬间崩塌，露出其作为权力共谋（complicity）工具的底色。

而这条新闻里最令人作呕的部分是 Laura Loomer 的“觉醒”。一个长期通过放大俄方宣传、辱骂乌克兰来获利的 MAGA 网红，在竞争对手 Candace Owens 抢占了“亲俄”生态位后，迅速通过一次实地访问完成了叙事转向。这根本不是什么“道德清晰”（moral clarity），而是一场典型的存在性战争博弈。她的转向不是基于对受害者的共情，而是基于对注意力资源（attention）的重新定价。在认知入口的争夺战中，当“亲俄”不再能带来最优解表达时，她迅速将“经历空袭”包装成新的政治资产。

至于那些被击中的仓库和死掉的平民，在这些权力玩家的博弈中，身体依然被降格为可消耗的工具。无论是欧盟的制裁大礼包还是乌克兰的无人机，本质上都是在用直接暴力（direct violence）去对冲结构暴力。而大众在阅读这类 briefing 时，很容易被这种“快餐式”的快讯掩盖掉一个事实：无论棋盘上的棋子怎么换，定义什么是“文明”与“正义”的解释权，依然在那些掌握着制裁名单和战争机器的男性手中。

The ousting of the FIDE president due to EU sanctions is a perfect irony. In the masculine-centric narrative, chess is curated as a realm of pure intellect and strategy. Yet, the reality is that the interpretative power of this field has always been monopolized by men like Dvorkovich, who hold high government offices. When structural violence—in the form of political sanctions—severs the links of capital and identity, the illusion of "pure reason" collapses, revealing its true nature as a tool for complicity.

The most repulsive part of this briefing is the "awakening" of Laura Loomer. A MAGA influencer who spent years amplifying Russian propaganda and abusing Ukrainians, she pivots her stance the moment her rival, Candace Owens, occupies the "pro-Russia" niche. This is not "moral clarity"; it is a textbook maneuver in an existential war. Her reversal isn't driven by empathy for victims, but by a re-pricing of attention. In the battle for cognitive entry points, when being pro-Russia no longer provides the optimal expression, she quickly packages "experiencing an air raid" as a new political asset.

As for the hit warehouses and dead civilians, bodies remain reduced to disposable tools in the games of these power players. Whether it is the EU's sanctions package or Ukraine's drones, it is simply using direct violence to hedge against structural violence. While reading these briefings, it's easy to let the "fast-food" nature of the news obscure one fact: regardless of how the pieces on the chessboard are swapped, the power to define "civilization" and "justice" still resides with the men who control the sanction lists and the war machines.
