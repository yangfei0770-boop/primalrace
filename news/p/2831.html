ICC检察官的陨落：权力顶端的性掠夺与共谋闭环The Fall of the ICC Prosecutor: Sexual Predation and the Loop of Complicity

性别 直接层 · 结构层 · 元暴力 The Guardian ↗ 2026-07-24 § 链接
最高级别的法律裁决者，往往是最高级别的性别暴力共谋者。
The highest arbiters of justice are often the most sophisticated accomplices of gender violence.

一个负责调查全球大屠杀和战争罪行的检察官，在自己的办公室里通过 coercive and non-consensual 的行为将下属客体化。这不是个案，而是典型的元暴力 (meta violence) 逻辑：在男性中心叙事中，掌控“正义”解释权的权力，本身就是一种可以被随意支配的性资源。当一个人在公共空间被定义为“正义的化身”时，他就在私密空间获得了完美的掩体。

最令人作呕的是这次 removal 耗时整整两年。从指控提出到最终投票，这期间的“纪律程序” (disciplinary process) 实际上是一次漫长的 structural violence。在等待投票结果的 700 多个日子里，受害者必须在一个由男性主导的官僚体系中，面对一个掌握全球司法话语权的强者，反复证明自己的痛苦。这种时间成本和心理损耗，本身就是权力对个体的二次掠夺。

Khan 的律师在指责程序“不公正” (procedurally unfair)。这种叙事非常滑稽——在父权结构中，所谓的“程序公正”通常是指给施暴者留多少缓冲地带，以及如何通过法律术语将掠夺包装成“误会”。

这次投票结果是 good_news 吗？在 direct 层面上，一个施暴者失去了席位，差额缩小了。但在 meta 层面上，这依然是男性内部的权力洗牌。如果 ICC 的机制不能从根本上拆除那个让“正义之神”在私下扮演“掠夺者”的结构，那么下一个被选出来的“正义之神”，可能只是一个更擅长掩盖痕迹的共谋者。

The prosecutor of the International Criminal Court, tasked with investigating global atrocities, turned his own office into a site of predation through coercive and non-consensual behavior. This is not an isolated incident, but a textbook manifestation of meta violence: the power to control the narrative of "justice" in the public sphere serves as a perfect camouflage for sexual exploitation in the private sphere.

The most repulsive detail is the two-year lag between the allegations and the removal. This "drawn-out disciplinary process" is a form of structural violence. For over 700 days, the victim had to navigate a male-dominated bureaucracy and prove her trauma to a man who wielded global judicial authority. The psychological attrition of this delay is a secondary plunder of the individual by the system.

Khan's lawyers claim the process was "procedurally unfair." This narrative is farcical. In a patriarchal structure, "procedural fairness" is typically a code for how much breathing room is granted to the perpetrator and how effectively predation can be rebranded as a "misunderstanding" through legal jargon.

Is this vote a piece of good_news? On a direct level, a predator lost his seat, narrowing the gap of violence. However, on a meta level, it remains a shuffle of power within a masculine-centric system. Unless the ICC dismantles the structure that allows a "God of Justice" to act as a predator in private, the next prosecutor may simply be a more skilled accomplice at erasing traces.
