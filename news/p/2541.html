葬礼上的导弹与被消解的“停火”scamMissiles at Funerals and the Ceasefire Scam

国际 直接层 · 结构层 · 文化层 · 元暴力 The Guardian ↗ 2026-07-17 § 链接
暴力不在于是否停火，而在于谁拥有定义“和平”的解释权。
Violence is not about the ceasefire, but about who controls the interpretation of "peace".

在加沙，一场葬礼变成了另一场屠杀的开端。死者在埋葬前任死者，而导弹精准地在此时降临。这种 direct violence 的残酷在于其循环性：暴力不再是达成某个政治目标的手段，而成了本身的目的。所谓的 ceasefire 只是一个 PR 版本的叙事，在 structural layer 上，资源分配与生存权的极度不对等依然存在，而这种不对等正是由一个强大的暴力机器在维持。

我们要警惕这种“停火后依然有死伤”的描述方式。当叙事将 1123 人的死亡与 5 名士兵的死亡放在同一个天平上对比时，这本身就是一种 cultural violence。它试图通过数量级的模糊化，将 genocide 伪装成某种“相互违约”的冲突。在这种 masculine-centric narrative 中，强者对弱者的单方面屠戮被简化为“响应违规”的博弈。这不仅是军事行动，更是对事实制造权的垄断。

谁在共谋？那些在国际场合通过签署协议来换取“和平”名声的政治精英，以及那些将这种不对称战争解读为“安全防御”的媒体。他们共同维护了一个元暴力机制：只要能给暴力贴上“正当防卫”或“反恐”的标签，那么在葬礼上杀人也就成了某种必要的代价。在这种叙事里，巴勒斯坦人的生命被客体化为可消耗的数字，而这种客体化正是原初种族被殖民的逻辑蓝图。

In Gaza, a funeral becomes the prologue to another massacre. The dead are being buried while missiles arrive to ensure the cycle continues. This direct violence is cruel not just in its execution, but in its circularity: violence is no longer a means to a political end, but the end itself. The so-called ceasefire is merely a PR version of reality; on the structural layer, the extreme inequality of resource distribution and survival rights persists, maintained by a dominant violence machine.

We must be wary of the narrative that describes casualties "after the ceasefire." When the deaths of 1,123 people are weighed against 5 soldiers in the same sentence, it is an act of cultural violence. It attempts to camouflage genocide as a "mutual violation" of a deal. In this masculine-centric narrative, the unilateral slaughter of the weak by the strong is reduced to a game of "responding to violations." This is not just military action; it is the monopoly over the manufacturing of facts.

Who are the complicitors? The political elites who trade signed agreements for a "peaceful" reputation, and the media that interpret this asymmetric warfare as "security defense." Together, they maintain a meta-violence mechanism: as long as violence is labeled as "self-defense" or "counter-terrorism," killing people at a funeral becomes a necessary cost. In this narrative, Palestinian lives are objectified into consumable numbers—the exact logical blueprint of the Primal Race being colonized.
