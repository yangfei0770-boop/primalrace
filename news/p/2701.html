用一个 McKinsey 伙伴来对冲 Lina Khan：一场典型的共谋表演Hedging Lina Khan with a McKinsey Partner: A Classic Performance of Complicity

国际 结构层 · 文化层 The New York Times ↗ 2026-07-22 § 链接
当“经济正义”被当作一种配比来对冲资本焦虑时，它就成了武器化的 PR。
When 'Economic Justice' becomes a hedge against capital anxiety, it is merely weaponized PR.

纽约市长 Mamdani 这次玩了一手极其熟练的“平衡术”。一边是代表强监管、试图打破垄断的 Lina Khan，另一边是来自全球咨询巨头 McKinsey 的 Anthony Shorris。在叙事层面，这被包装成“经济发展与经济正义的融合”，但从博弈论来看，这不过是在结构性暴力面前的一次表演性让步。

这种人事安排本质上是在建立一个共谋机制。引入 McKinsey 的伙伴作为总裁，实际上是给商业社区递交的一份投名状，旨在告诉资本：即便有“经济正义”的口号，实际的资源分配逻辑依然在旧有的精英共谋网络之中。Lina Khan 的存在提供了一种“进步”的视觉表达，而 Shorris 则确保了这种表达不会真正触碰到结构性利益的底线。

这就是典型的文化暴力伪装：通过创造一个看似矛盾的组合，让外界相信“正义”已经进入了决策层。但实际上，当“正义”需要被一个 McKinsey 伙伴来“平衡”时，它就已经被客体化为一种可量化的、用来安抚底层支持者的筹码。这种“融合”不是 Actual 朝 Potential 走近，而是在维持现状的同时，通过更新叙事入口来掩盖权力的垄断。

真正的经济正义应该是对资源分配权的彻底重构，而不是在一个由资本定义规则的 EDC 机构里，增加一个监管者的席位。这场博弈的赢家依然是那些能够定义什么是“增长”的人，而所谓的“正义”，只是被允许在特定的范围内进行一次温顺的表达。

Mayor Mamdani is executing a textbook maneuver in the art of balancing. On one side, he has Lina Khan, the champion of strong regulation; on the other, Anthony Shorris, a partner from the global consulting giant McKinsey. While the narrative frames this as a "blend of economic development and economic justice," from a game-theory perspective, it is nothing more than a performative concession in the face of structural violence.

This appointment is essentially the construction of a complicity mechanism. Bringing in a McKinsey partner as president is a signal to the business community—a pledge that despite the rhetoric of "economic justice," the actual logic of resource allocation remains within the old elite networks of complicity. Lina Khan provides the visual expression of "progress," while Shorris ensures that this expression never truly threatens the structural bottom line.

This is cultural violence in its most sophisticated form: creating a seemingly contradictory pairing to convince the public that "justice" has entered the room. However, when "justice" requires a McKinsey partner to "balance" it, it has been objectified into a quantifiable chip used to appease grassroots supporters. This "blend" is not the Actual moving toward the Potential; it is the maintenance of the status quo through a revamped narrative entrance to mask the monopoly of power.

True economic justice would require a total reconstruction of the power to distribute resources, not simply adding a regulator's seat to an EDC agency whose rules are still defined by capital. The winners of this game remain those who define "growth," while "justice" is merely permitted a docile expression within a controlled perimeter.
