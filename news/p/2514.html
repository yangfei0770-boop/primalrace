AI 泡沫的崩塌：一场关于“定义现实”的定价权博弈The AI Bubble Burst: A Game of Pricing Power and Defined Realities

科技 结构层 · 文化层 The New York Times ↗ 2026-07-17 § 链接
当资本发现无法通过定义新定律来掩盖能力缺失时，泡沫即刻转化为暴力。
When capital realizes it can no longer mask incapacity by redefining laws, the bubble instantly converts into violence.

这场全球性的抛售不是简单的“投资者焦虑”，而是一次认知入口的集体坍塌。长期以来，AI 巨头们通过构建一套关于“世界改变可能性”的武器化叙事，成功地将庞大的资本投入包装成某种不可逆的进化。但这本质上是一场关于定价权的 scam：用一个尚未实现的 Future Potential 来抵消当前的 Actual 亏损，并诱导共谋者（投资者）共同维持这个虚高的估值。

Moonshot 发布 Kimi K3 宣称达到美国巨头水平，这恰恰触发了最残酷的博弈逻辑。当一个所谓的“顶峰”被一个更廉价的开源模型通过“定义相同”来消解时，原本由稀缺性支撑的估值逻辑就失效了。这就像我在分析华为 $\tau$-scaling 时提到的：如果现实不配合，就试图重新定义现实。但资本市场最诚实的地方在于，它只承认能转化为 revenue 的能力，而不承认被宣称的“定律”。

目前 AI 产业的结构是：无数公司在借贷中通过建设数据中心来堆砌“存在感”，这是一种典型的结构性暴力。这种暴力在泡沫期被掩盖在“科技进步”的文化外壳下，但当 Potential 和 Actual 的差额大到无法用叙事填补时，崩盘就是唯一的公正表达。所谓的“焦虑”，不过是共谋者们在意识到自己被当作燃料后，试图在撤离时抢占最后一点流动性的恐慌。

This global sell-off is not mere "investor anxiety," but a collective collapse of the cognitive entrance. For too long, AI giants have weaponized a narrative of "world-changing possibilities" to frame massive capital burns as an inevitable evolution. In essence, this was a pricing power scam: using a hypothetical Future Potential to offset Actual losses, coaxing complicitors (investors) to maintain an inflated valuation.

Moonshot’s release of Kimi K3, claiming parity with US giants, triggered the most brutal game logic. When a perceived "peak" is dissolved by a cheaper open-source model claiming "equivalence," the valuation logic based on scarcity fails. It is exactly what I noted with Huawei’s $\tau$-scaling: when reality doesn't cooperate, they try to redefine reality. But the capital market is honest in one regard—it only recognizes capability that converts to revenue, not "laws" that are merely declared.

The current AI structural framework is one where companies pile up "existence" via borrowed funds for data centers—a clear form of structural violence. This violence was masked by a cultural shell of "technological progress," but when the gap between Potential and Actual becomes too wide for narratives to bridge, a crash is the only Just Expression. The so-called "anxiety" is simply the panic of complicitors realizing they were the fuel, scrambling for liquidity before the exit closes.
