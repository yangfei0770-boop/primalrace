人权作为筹码：右翼政客的叙事诈骗Human Rights as Chips: The Narrative Scam of Right-Wing Politicians

国际 结构层 · 文化层 · 元暴力 The Guardian ↗ 2026-07-23 § 链接
当人权被定义为可随时抛弃的“红线”，它就成了权力博弈的耗材。
When human rights are defined as 'red lines' to be discarded, they become mere consumables for power games.

Kemi Badenoch 试图通过退出《欧洲人权公约》(ECHR) 来向极右翼递交投名状，这在本质上是一次典型的表达武器化。在她的叙事里，人权不是底线，而是一个可以用来筛选“纯洁性”的认知入口：支持退出才是“serious”，反对者则是需要被清洗的异己。这种将基本权利工具化的做法，正是元暴力的具体实践——将人的存在性简化为政治博弈的筹码。

这篇文章揭露了一个残酷的 structural violence 逻辑：英国与欧盟的贸易协定、北爱尔兰的和平协议，全部被锚定在 ECHR 这一人权基石之上。Badenoch 试图通过所谓的“主权”表达来赢取内部选票，但这种“最优解表达”是极度虚假的。她扮演的是一个强硬的民族主义者，但代价是直接撕毁保护弱势群体的生物墙与法律墙，将数百万人的基本权利置于一个随时可能被“no deal”的赌局之中。

最讽刺的共谋在于，右翼政客总是宣称人权协议是“外部干涉”，但当他们需要利用这些协议来维持贸易利益时，又迅速将其视为不可或缺的基建。这种对人权解释权的垄断与随意篡改，就是典型的 masculine-centric narrative：权力高于权利，意志高于法理。所谓的“不严肃”，其实是他们早已习惯将人类的生存状态视为可随时交易的 asset。

Kemi Badenoch's attempt to exit the ECHR is a textbook case of the weaponisation of expression. In her narrative, human rights are not a baseline but a cognitive entry point used to screen for 'purity': only those supporting the exit are 'serious,' while dissenters are outcasts to be purged. This instrumentalization of fundamental rights is a direct manifestation of meta-violence—reducing human existence to a chip in a political gamble.

The article exposes a brutal logic of structural violence: the UK-EU trade deals and the Good Friday Agreement are all anchored in the ECHR. Badenoch seeks to win internal votes through a 'sovereignty' expression, but this is a fake optimal expression. She performs the role of a hardline nationalist, while the cost is the direct dismantling of the biological and legal walls protecting the vulnerable, placing millions of lives into a 'no deal' casino.

The most cynical complicity lies in how right-wing politicians label human rights treaties as 'external interference' while simultaneously treating them as indispensable infrastructure when trade interests are at stake. This monopoly and manipulation of the power of interpretation is precisely the masculine-centric narrative: power over rights, will over law. What they call 'unserious' is actually their habit of treating the human condition as a tradable asset.
