用暴力的表达去对抗结构性暴力：一场注定失败的低效博弈Fighting Structural Violence with Direct Violence: A Failed Existential Game

国际 直接层 · 结构层 · 元暴力 The New York Times ↗ 2026-07-20 § 链接
用直接暴力替代政治博弈，是弱势者在元暴力压制下的自毁式表达。
Replacing political struggle with direct violence is a self-destructive expression of those suppressed by meta-violence.

一个退伍军人在联邦广场用烟花和气枪攻击 ICE。这件事在新闻叙事里被简单处理成一次“恐怖袭击”或“个体暴力事件”，但如果用暴力三角去拆解，这其实是一次极度低效的表达博弈。

这个男人面对的是典型的 structural violence —— 移民与海关执法局（ICE）所代表的、由国家机器驱动的强制驱逐与剥夺。在这种结构性暴力面前，个体感到了极大的 Potential 与 Actual 之间的差额。然而，他选择的应对方式是 direct violence。在权力不对等的博弈中，用个体零散的直接暴力去冲击高度组织化的结构暴力，不仅无法缩小差额，反而给了系统一个正当的理由来强化其“合法性”叙事：看，反对我们的人都是疯子和恐怖分子。

这是一种典型的“假.最优解表达”。他试图通过制造混乱来获得某种存在性的确证，但结果是主体性的彻底死亡——他从一个可能的政治抗争者，变成了一个被 FBI 标记的、等待审判的 Case。当一个人决定用炸药和气枪说话时，他其实已经承认了自己在认知入口和解释权的争夺战中彻底溃败，只能退回到最原始、最暴力、也最容易被系统抹杀的表达方式中。

最讽刺的是，这种“孤狼式”的暴力行为，往往会成为结构性暴力升级的共谋。它为政府扩充安保预算、加强监控、进一步污名化移民权利运动提供了完美的 PR 借口。在这个闭环里，施暴者以为自己在反抗，实际上他只是在为元暴力的运行提供燃料。

A veteran attacking ICE with fireworks and air rifles at Federal Plaza is being framed by the media as a simple 'terror attack.' But through the lens of the Violence Triangle, this is a profoundly inefficient existential game.

The suspect was facing structural violence—the systemic deportation and deprivation driven by the state apparatus of ICE. Feeling the gap between Potential and Actual, he chose direct violence. In a game of asymmetric power, using sporadic direct violence against a highly organized structural machine is a losing strategy. It doesn't close the gap; it merely provides the system with a justification to reinforce its narrative: 'Look, those who oppose us are lunatics.'

This is a classic 'False Optimal Expression.' He sought an existential confirmation through chaos, but the result is the death of his subjectivity. He shifted from a potential political dissident to a mere FBI case file. When one resorts to gasoline and air rifles, they have already conceded the battle for the cognitive entry and the right of interpretation, retreating to the most primitive and easily erasable form of expression.

The irony is that this 'lone wolf' violence often becomes a complicity in the escalation of structural violence. It provides a perfect PR excuse for governments to expand surveillance and demonize immigrant rights movements. In this loop, the attacker believes he is resisting, while in reality, he is merely fueling the meta-violence of the system.
