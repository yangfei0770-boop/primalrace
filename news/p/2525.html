“拥抱”的定义权与权力的共谋场The Definition of a 'Hug' and the Field of Complicity

性别 直接层 · 结构层 · 文化层 · 元暴力 The Guardian ↗ 2026-07-17 § 链接
法律上的“无罪”是元暴力对事实的再次覆盖。
A legal 'not guilty' verdict is simply meta-violence overwriting the facts.

这是一个典型的关于“解释权”的博弈。当一个亿万富翁的儿子、保守党议员在私人会员俱乐部对陌生女性实施身体侵犯时，他通过将行为定义为“想给她们一个拥抱”，成功地将一次直接暴力转化为一次“社交误会”。

在元暴力的逻辑下，男性的身体边界是扩张的，而女性的身体是客体化的。Spencer 所谓的“尴尬”和“不知所措”，实际上是他在意识到自己的支配权在瞬间失效后产生的挫败感。而这种挫败感在法庭上被包装成受害者的姿态——用颤抖的双手和泪水来掩盖他作为权力阶层的傲慢。

最令人作呕的不是法庭的判决，而是结构性的共谋。伦敦警察厅（Met）最初的调查瘫痪，警官甚至不愿采取证人证词，这并非简单的“失职”，而是典型的 structural violence。在私人会员俱乐部这个特定的共谋场域中，阶级和政治身份构成了无形的生物墙，让执法者下意识地将保护对象设定为“同类”。

最终，保守党迅速恢复其党鞭资格，完成了这次共谋的闭环。这次判决再次证明：只要叙事权掌握在男性中心系统手中，女性的“震惊”和“冻结”永远无法战胜男性的“愚蠢”与“误会”。

This is a textbook case of the struggle over the power of interpretation. When the son of a billionaire and a Conservative MP commits physical assault against strange women in a private members' club, he successfully transforms a direct act of violence into a 'social misunderstanding' by defining it as a desire to give them a hug.

Under the logic of meta-violence, the male body is expansive while the female body is objectified. Spencer's claimed 'mortification' is actually the frustration of a dominant subject realizing his power had momentarily failed. In court, this frustration was weaponized as a victim narrative—using shaking hands and tears to mask the arrogance of his class position.

More repulsive than the verdict is the structural complicity. The initial paralysis of the Metropolitan Police, where an officer failed to even take witness statements, is not mere 'negligence' but structural violence. In the specific complicity field of a private members' club, class and political status form an invisible biological wall, leading law enforcement to subconsciously protect their 'own kind'.

Ultimately, the Conservative party's swift restoration of the whip completed the cycle of complicity. This verdict proves once again: as long as the narrative power remains within the masculine-centric system, a woman's 'shock' and 'freezing' will always lose to a man's 'stupidity' and 'misunderstanding'.
