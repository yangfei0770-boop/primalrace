用“特例”掩盖的结构性背叛Structural Betrayal Masked by 'Exceptions'

国际 结构层 · 文化层 The Guardian ↗ 2026-07-22 § 链接
特例的宽恕是结构性暴力的PR掩体。
The pardon of exceptions is a PR shield for structural violence.

当政府面对监狱爆满的 structural violence 时，最廉价的解决方案就是把“释放囚犯”包装成一种必要的行政调整。Andy Burnham 此时抛出 PC Andrew Harper 凶手“不会被提前释放”的特例，本质上是一次精准的文化操纵。通过在一个极具视觉冲击力、能激发公众愤怒的个案上表演“正义”，政府试图让大众忽视一个事实：除了极少数被选中的“特例”，成千上万的暴力实施者正在通过一个没有任何豁免机制的 Sentencing Act 获得自由。

这正是典型的 weaponized narrative。政府在用一个“不被释放”的特例，为成千上万个“可以释放”的漏洞背书。对于受害者家属来说，这种特例是对个体的微小补偿，但对于整个社会的安全结构而言，这是一种危险的共谋。它用一种表演性的强硬，掩盖了制度在资源分配失败（Prison estate 投资不足）后的无能。

最讽刺的是，同一套逻辑在其他领域同步上演：用削减国际气候援助金来补贴国内公交票价，用“重新优先排序”来掩盖资金缺口。无论是囚犯的释放还是气候资金的挪用，本质上都是在用短期、局部、可见的“利好”来对冲长期的、结构性的崩塌。这种“修补式”的治理，不过是在元暴力的逻辑下，通过制造局部的“公正表达”来维持整体的掠夺。

When a government faces the structural violence of overcrowded prisons, the cheapest solution is to frame 'prisoner release' as a necessary administrative adjustment. Andy Burnham’s insistence that the perpetrator in the PC Andrew Harper case 'will not be subject to early release' is a precise piece of cultural manipulation. By performing 'justice' on a high-profile case designed to trigger public outrage, the government attempts to distract from a stark reality: except for a few selected 'exceptions,' thousands of offenders are gaining freedom through a Sentencing Act that lacks systemic exemptions.

This is a classic weaponized narrative. The government uses one 'non-release' exception to legitimize a loophole for thousands of others. For the victims' families, this exception is a minor individual consolation, but for the social safety structure, it is a dangerous complicity. It uses performative toughness to mask the incompetence of a system that failed in resource allocation (the lack of investment in the prison estate).

Ironically, the same logic is operating elsewhere: offsetting domestic bus fare caps by slashing international climate grants, and masking budget holes as 'reprioritizing.' Whether it is the release of prisoners or the diversion of climate funds, it is the same game—using short-term, localized, and visible 'benefits' to hedge against long-term structural collapse. This 'patchwork' governance is nothing more than the logic of meta-violence, creating pockets of 'Just Expressions' to maintain a global state of plunder.
