1.5万亿美元的账单：AWS在用Glitch测试你的心率The $1.5 Trillion Bill: AWS Testing Your Heart Rate via Glitch

科技 结构层 · 文化层 The Guardian ↗ 2026-07-17 § 链接
技术故障是掩体，结构性权力对个体的绝对支配才是真相。
Technical glitches are masks; the truth is the absolute dominance of structural power over individuals.

AWS这次的 global glitch 是一场极其荒诞的权力演示。当一个学生或小慈善机构在账单上看到 1.5 万亿美元或 100 亿美元时，他们经历的不是对“计算错误”的困惑，而是一种瞬间被剥夺存在感的恐惧。这种恐惧源于一个事实：在当前的结构性暴力 (structural violence) 中，大科技公司掌握着定义你生存成本的绝对定价权。你习惯于每月支付几美分，但系统只要轻轻拨动一个 unit pricing 的开关，就能在瞬间将你定义为全球最大的债务人。

最讽刺的共谋 (complicity) 在于，AWS 习惯于将自己包装成 "customer-centric" 的服务者，但这次故障撕开了伪装。它揭示了用户与平台之间极不对等且脆弱的博弈关系：用户在极低的价格诱饵下将数据和业务迁移到云端，实际上是进入了一个由 Jeff Bezos 定义的黑盒。当 glitch 发生时，用户唯一的反应是 "soul left my body" 或 "heart attack"，这种生理性的崩溃正是结构性压迫在个体身上的具象化表达。

AWS 用一个半小时地解决了 "computation subsystem" 的问题，但它无法解决它给数百万用户制造的心理创伤。这种 "confusion and concern" 的道歉是典型的文化暴力 (cultural violence) —— 用轻描淡写的词汇掩盖其基础设施对个体生活具有毁灭性影响的能力。如果一个系统能随手开出万亿美金的账单，那么它在正常运行时对你数据的解释权和操控权，本身就是一种潜伏的暴力。

The recent global glitch at AWS is a surreal demonstration of power. When a student or a small charity sees a bill for $1.5 trillion or $10 billion, they aren't just confused by a 'calculation error'—they experience a sudden, total erasure of their existential security. This panic stems from a stark reality of structural violence: Big Tech holds the absolute pricing power to define the cost of your existence. You are accustomed to paying a few cents, but the system, by merely flipping a unit pricing switch, can instantly redefine you as the world's largest debtor.

The most cynical complicity lies in AWS's branding as a "customer-centric" company. This glitch strips away the mask, revealing the profoundly asymmetrical game between user and platform. Users, lured by low costs, migrated their data to the cloud, effectively entering a black box defined by Jeff Bezos. When the glitch hit, the only reaction was "soul left my body" or "heart attack"—a physiological collapse that is the concrete expression of structural oppression.

AWS fixed its "computation subsystem" in ninety minutes, but it cannot fix the psychological trauma inflicted on millions. Their apology for "confusion and concern" is a textbook example of cultural violence—using sanitized language to mask the fact that their infrastructure possesses the power to destroy individual lives. If a system can casually generate a trillion-dollar bill, then its control over your data and its power of interpretation during 'normal' operation is, in itself, a latent form of violence.
