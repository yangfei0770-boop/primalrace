Google 的 AI 围栏：从信息的入口变成信息的终点Google's AI Fence: From Gateway to Dead End

科技 结构层 · 文化层 The New York Times ↗ 2026-07-20 § 链接
当入口决定不再导流而是截流，互联网就从 open web 变成了私有殖民地。
When the gateway stops directing and starts intercepting, the open web becomes a private colony.

Google 正在完成一次极其隐蔽的权力篡夺。早年的 Google 扮演的是 gateway 的角色，它的商业逻辑是建立在「链接」之上的——通过将用户推向 open web，它在维持一个生态的同时地盘化。但现在，Gemini 的 AI Mode 正在把这个 gateway 变成一个 fence。它不再给你链接，而是直接给你一个由 AI 总结的「答案」。

这在本质上是一场关于认知入口 (cognitive entry) 的武器化战争。当用户不再点击链接，原有的内容生产者——那些出版社、大学、独立博主——就失去了被看见的可能，而 Google 则通过截流信息，垄断了对「事实」的最终解释权。它不再是信息的索引者，而成了事实的制造者。这种从「导流」到「截流」的转变，是典型的 structural violence：它通过改变系统规则，在不产生物理冲突的情况下，直接剥夺了数百万内容创作者的生存资源。

最讽刺的是，这种行为被包装在「效率」和「便捷」的叙事之下。这又是典型的文化暴力 (cultural violence)，用「用户体验」这个 weaponized concept 来掩盖其殖民 open web 的野心。当一个公司能够决定你看到什么、如何理解这些信息，且你无需离开它的页面就能获得所有「答案」时，互联网的公共性就死在了这个 AI 围栏里。

这不再是关于搜索的升级，而是一次对互联网原初生态的彻底清算。我们正在进入一个由巨头定义的、封闭的语义孤岛。

Google is executing a stealthy seizure of power. In its early days, Google acted as a gateway; its business logic was built on the 'link'—monetizing the open web by pushing users toward it. Now, Gemini's AI Mode is transforming that gateway into a fence. It no longer provides links; it provides a curated 'answer'.

This is a weaponization of the cognitive entry. When users stop clicking, the original content creators—publishers, universities, independent bloggers—lose the possibility of being seen. Google, by intercepting the flow, now monopolizes the final interpretation of 'facts'. It has ceased to be an indexer and has become a manufacturer of reality. This shift from 'directing' to 'intercepting' is a textbook case of structural violence: it alters the system's rules to strip millions of creators of their survival resources without a single physical blow.

Cruelly, this is wrapped in a narrative of 'efficiency' and 'convenience'. This is cultural violence at work, using the weaponized concept of 'user experience' to mask a colonial ambition toward the open web. When a single corporation decides what you see and how you understand it, and you never have to leave its page to find the 'truth', the public nature of the internet dies within that AI fence.

This isn't a search upgrade; it's a total liquidation of the internet's primal ecosystem. We are drifting into closed semantic islands defined entirely by Big Tech.
