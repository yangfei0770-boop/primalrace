用 950 亿美金在叙事里扮演“救世主”Playing the 'Savior' with a $95 Billion Price Tag

国际 结构层 · 文化层 · 元暴力 The New York Times ↗ 2026-07-22 § 链接
战争预算是男性中心叙事最昂贵的武器化表达。
War budgets are the most expensive weaponized expressions of the masculine-centric narrative.

950 亿美金。这个数字在 House 的投票中被简化为“支持我们的士兵”，这正是典型的 weaponized expression。通过将巨大的财政支出包装成“士兵的需求”，共和党成功地把一场充满政治算计的战争，转化为一种关于忠诚、勇气与保护的浪漫主义叙事。在这种叙事里，战争本身消失了，取而代之的是一个巨大的、被神化的“保护者”形象。

注意这个预算的结构：730 亿给军队，100 亿给投票限制。这本质上是一次精准的权力共谋。用战争的宏大叙事作为掩体，悄悄地在 structural 层级上通过限制投票权来剥夺他者的政治表达。当大众在讨论“爱国”和“士兵”时，权力中心正在高效地完成对认知入口的封锁。这种“先用战争制造危机，再用危机收拢权力”的逻辑，是典型的 masculine-centric narrative 在运作。

最讽刺的是，这种所谓的“保护”从来不包含被战争波及的平民，尤其是女性。在这些由男性制定、由男性投票、由男性执行的预算案中，女性的身体和生命永远是 invisible 的。她们要么是战争叙事中被用来正当化侵略的“受难者”，要么就是被结构性暴力直接抹除的背景板。所谓的“文明”预算，不过是给元暴力穿上的一件昂贵西装。

95 billion dollars. In the House vote, this figure was reduced to "supporting our troops," a textbook case of weaponized expression. By packaging massive fiscal expenditure as "the needs of soldiers," Republicans have transformed a calculated political war into a romanticized narrative of loyalty, courage, and protection. In this framing, the war itself vanishes, replaced by a magnified, sanctified image of the "Protector."

Look at the structure: $73 billion for the military, $10 billion for voting restrictions. This is a precise act of complicity. Using the grand narrative of war as a shield, they are quietly stripping others of their political expression at the structural level by restricting voting rights. While the public is distracted by discourse on "patriotism," the power center is efficiently sealing the cognitive entry points. This logic—manufacturing a crisis through war to consolidate power—is the masculine-centric narrative in full operation.

The irony is that this "protection" never extends to the civilians caught in the crossfire, especially women. In budgets drafted, voted on, and executed by men, female bodies and lives remain invisible. They are either the "victims" used to justify aggression in a weaponized narrative or the background noise erased by structural violence. This so-called "civilized" budget is nothing more than an expensive suit tailored for meta-violence.
