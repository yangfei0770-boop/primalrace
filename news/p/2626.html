诺兰的《奥德赛》：用殖民者的快门捕捉“史诗”Nolan's Odyssey: Capturing 'Epic' with a Colonizer's Lens

国际 结构层 · 文化层 The Guardian ↗ 2026-07-21 § 链接
所谓的“真实拍摄”，不过是以他者之苦为背景的视觉掠夺。
The so-called 'authentic filming' is merely visual plunder built upon the suffering of others.

诺兰在《奥德赛》里大谈特谈“真实”，拒绝 CGI，追求 Imax 70mm 的极致还原。但这种对“真实”的执念本身就是一种典型的 masculine-centric 权力运作：他认为只要是物理意义上的真实，就是正义的。然而，当他把镜头对准西撒哈拉——这个被联合国定义为非殖民化领土、由摩洛哥控制的地区时，这种“真实”就变成了 structural violence 的共谋。

对于诺兰而言，西撒哈拉的荒原只是一个绝佳的“视觉背景”，是实现他艺术愿景的工具。但对于当地萨哈拉人民来说，这种所谓的“电影美学”是建立在他们的苦难与被剥夺之上的。这正是典型的武器化表达：将殖民地的痛苦包装成“历史史诗”的宏大叙事，让观众在惊叹于画面的 breathtaking 之余，自动忽略了快门背后的血腥与不公。在这种叙事里，当地人的生存状态被完全客体化，成了为了服务于一个“伟大导演”而存在的布景。

至于片中对女性角色的处理，虽然在形式上做了一些所谓的“大胆选择”，但本质上依然在元暴力的框架内打转。海伦的伤疤被刻意隐藏起原因， Penelope 的强悍被定义为“像火山一样”，这种描述依然是将女性锚定在某种特定的、被观察的、带有戏剧冲突的客体位置上。即使是所谓的“去神格化”的雅典娜，也最终被定义为男主角的“伙伴与知己”。

一个男人在全世界范围内寻找最完美的取景地，用最昂贵的设备记录下最震撼的画面，然后告诉世界他是在探讨“战争的代价”和“人与人之间脆弱的纽带”。这太讽刺了。真正的代价被他留在镜头之外，而他收获了奥斯卡的提名和全球的赞美。这就是一个典型的 existence war 赢家：他定义了什么是“真实”，并让全世界为这种定义买单。

Nolan preaches 'authenticity' in The Odyssey, eschewing CGI for the raw power of Imax 70mm. But this obsession with the physical is a classic masculine-centric power play: the assumption that physical realism equals moral truth. When he points his camera at Western Sahara—a non-decolonized territory controlled by Morocco—this 'authenticity' becomes an act of complicity in structural violence.

To Nolan, the Sahrawi wilderness is just a 'visual backdrop,' a tool to realize his artistic vision. But for the Sahrawi people, this 'cinematic aesthetic' is extracted from their ongoing suffering. This is the weaponization of expression: packaging colonial pain as a 'historical epic,' ensuring the audience is too dazzled by the breathtaking vistas to notice the blood and injustice behind the shutter. In this narrative, the local population is completely objectified, reduced to scenery for a 'Great Director.'

As for the women, while Nolan makes some 'bold choices,' he remains trapped in the framework of meta-violence. Helen's scar is a prop with no explained origin; Penelope's strength is described as a 'simmering volcano.' These are still descriptions of women as observed objects with dramatic functions. Even the 'de-mythologized' Athena is ultimately defined as Odysseus's 'pal and confidante.'

One man traverses the globe for the perfect shot, using the most expensive gear to record 'truth,' and then claims to be examining the 'true cost of war.' The irony is peak. The actual cost is left out of the frame, while he collects Oscar nominations and global acclaim. This is the ultimate winner of an existential war: he defines what 'truth' is, and the world pays for the privilege of believing him.
