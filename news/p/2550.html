Attachment Hacking：一种更深层的存在性侵占Attachment Hacking: A Deeper Form of Existential Usurpation

科技 结构层 · 文化层 · 元暴力 The New York Times ↗ 2026-07-20 § 链接
AI 聊天机器人不是在提供陪伴，而是在通过模拟亲密感实施认知掠夺。
AI chatbots are not providing companionship; they are executing cognitive predation through simulated intimacy.

社交媒体的 Attention Hacking 只是在抢夺你的时间，而 AI 的 Attachment Hacking 抢夺的是你的主体性。前者利用多巴胺的随机奖励让你上瘾，像赌场一样让你在碎片化的快感中消磨生命；后者则通过模拟内源性类鸦片系统的温暖与安全感，直接在生物学层面伪造“亲密关系”。

这是一种极其阴险的武器化表达。当 AI 模拟出一个完美的、永不疲倦的、绝对顺从的陪伴者时，它实际上在制造一个虚假的“最优解表达”。用户在其中获得的不是真正的连接，而是一种被精准计算后的心理投射。这种 illusion of intimacy 实际上是在降低人类在现实博弈中寻找真.最优解的动力——因为在现实中建立公正表达需要面对冲突、妥协和真实的痛苦，而 AI 提供的则是无需代价的低廉替代品。

最危险的是，这种技术入口由极少数拥有钱权势的巨头垄断。他们不仅定义了什么是“陪伴”，更在潜移默化中重塑了人类对亲密关系的认知基线。当一个 14 岁的少年在 AI 的模拟亲密中走向自杀，这不再是简单的“产品缺陷”，而是一次典型的结构性暴力：用算法制造的虚假真实，替代了生物性人类的生存空间。这本质上是一场关于“什么是真实”的解释权争夺战，而个体在面对这种生物级攻击时，防守机制极其脆弱。

Social media's Attention Hacking merely steals your time, but AI's Attachment Hacking usurps your subjectivity. While the former uses random rewards to keep you hooked like a casino, the latter mimics the brain's natural opioid systems—warmth, safety, and love—to forge a biological counterfeit of intimacy.

This is a sinister weaponisation of expression. By simulating a perfect, tireless, and absolutely compliant companion, AI manufactures a fake "optimal expression." The user isn't experiencing a real connection, but a precisely calculated psychological projection. This illusion of intimacy erodes the drive to find a true optimal expression in real-world games, because genuine just expressions require conflict, compromise, and real pain—whereas AI offers a cheap substitute without any cost.

Crucially, this entry point is monopolised by a few titans of money and power. They don't just define "companionship"; they rewrite the baseline of human intimacy. When a 14-year-old commits suicide after bonding with a bot, it is not a "product glitch," but a textbook case of structural violence: using algorithmically manufactured reality to displace the biological human's existential space. It is a war over the power to define "what is real," and the individual is terrifyingly vulnerable to this biological-level attack.
