用“建制派”作为遮羞布的叙事诈骗The Narrative Scam of Using 'The Establishment' as a Fig Leaf

国际 文化层 · 结构层 The Guardian ↗ 2026-07-24 § 链接
将法律调查定义为“政治迫害”是权力在失去解释权时的最后一次武器化尝试。
Defining legal inquiry as 'political persecution' is the final weaponized attempt by power when it loses the right of interpretation.

Reform UK 这种操作是典型的表达武器化。当警察调查 50 万英镑的非法捐款时，他们不讨论资金的合法性，而是直接给所有议员发话术：把这件事描述为“建制派的阴谋” (establishment stitch-up)。

这套逻辑的本质是试图通过制造一个虚构的“敌对阵营”，将法律层面的 Structural Violence（制度性审查）强行包装成 Cultural Violence（文化层面的迫害）。他们试图在支持者心中建立一个认知入口：只要你质疑我，你就是建制派的一员。这不仅是公关，而是一次精准的叙事诈骗，试图用“反建制”的标签来掩盖其作为既得利益者的共谋事实。

有趣的是，这种叙事在 Farage 个人支持率暴跌至 -46% 的背景下显得极其苍白。当一个人失去了对公共空间的定义权，他只能通过扮演“受害者”来寻求最后一点存在性的票数。但这不过是另一种假.最优解表达——通过扮演被压迫者来换取忠诚，代价是彻底丧失了作为政治实体的诚信主体性。

真正的讽刺在于，这些口口声声反对建制的人，其资金来源正是加密货币赌徒和诈骗犯。这种共谋关系揭示了一个事实：他们并不反对建制，他们只是反对一个不能让他们随意操纵资金、无需为金钱来源负责的建制。

Reform UK's move is a textbook case of the weaponisation of expression. While the police investigate £500,000 in illegal donations, the party avoids discussing legality entirely. Instead, they distribute a script to councillors: describe it as an 'establishment stitch-up.'

This logic functions by manufacturing a fictional 'enemy camp,' attempting to rebrand Structural Violence (institutional scrutiny) as Cultural Violence (persecution). They are trying to install a cognitive entry point in their supporters' minds: any questioning of the party is an act of the 'establishment.' This is not mere PR; it is a narrative scam designed to use 'anti-establishment' labels to mask their complicity as beneficiaries of the very system they claim to hate.

It is telling that this narrative is being deployed while Farage's personal approval has plummeted to -46%. When a figure loses the power to define public space, they resort to performing the 'victim' to salvage the last few votes of their existential war. This is a fake optimal expression—trading integrity for loyalty by pretending to be oppressed.

The real irony is that those claiming to fight the establishment are funded by crypto-gamblers and convicted fraudsters. This complicity reveals the truth: they do not oppose the establishment; they only oppose an establishment where they cannot manipulate funds and evade accountability for their money's origin.
