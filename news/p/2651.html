政治进化论：一种关于生存最优解的精准换皮Political Evolution: A Precise Skin-Changing of the Optimal Expression

国际 文化层 · 结构层 The New York Times ↗ 2026-07-21 § 链接
所谓的“进化”往往不是良心的觉醒，而是博弈中最优解的迁移。
So-called "evolution" is rarely a moral awakening, but a migration of the optimal expression in a game of power.

把一个政客从“反堕胎”到“进步派”的转变描述为“remarkable evolution”，是典型的媒体叙事 scam。在政治博弈场中，不存在所谓的灵魂觉醒，只有对权力席位和认知入口的精准计算。Troy Jackson 的路径极其清晰：在 90 年代的伐木工社区，反堕胎是进入该阵营的投名状，是当时的“最优解表达”；而当他的政治野心上升，面对民主党的主流叙事和更广阔的选民基础时，他迅速地将自己的表达进行了“换皮”。

这种转变本质上是他在存在性战争中为了维持其“票”的价值而进行的策略性调整。他所谓的“意识到错误”，实际上是意识到旧的表达已经不再能为他提供结构性优势。从一个反堕胎组织的满分评分到计划生育协会的满分评分，这不是价值观的漂移，而是对不同权力共谋节点的精准切换。

最讽刺的是，这种“进步”被包装成一种个人成长故事。当一个男性政客通过在立法中给女性提供堕胎权来获得“进步派”标签时，他实际上是在利用女性的结构性困境来完成自己的政治资本积累。这种好消息的表象下，隐藏的是一个极其冷酷的逻辑：女性的身体权利在此时成了他晋升 Senate 的筹码。这种胜利是表演性的，而非结构性的，因为他的主体性从未改变——他依然是一个在不同叙事间跳转的权力操盘手。

Describing a politician's shift from "anti-abortion" to "progressive" as a "remarkable evolution" is a classic media narrative scam. In the arena of political gaming, there is no such thing as a spiritual awakening, only a precise calculation of power seats and cognitive entries. Troy Jackson's path is crystal clear: in the logging communities of the 90s, being anti-abortion was the password to enter that camp, the "optimal expression" of the time. As his political ambitions grew and he faced the mainstream Democratic narrative, he swiftly "reskinned" his expression.

This transition is essentially a strategic adjustment to maintain the value of his "vote" in the existential war. His claim of "realizing mistakes" is actually a realization that the old expression no longer provided a structural advantage. Moving from a perfect rating by an anti-abortion group to a perfect rating by Planned Parenthood is not a drift in values, but a precise switch between different nodes of complicity.

The irony lies in how this "progress" is packaged as a story of personal growth. When a male politician gains a "progressive" label by granting abortion rights in legislation, he is effectively leveraging the structural predicament of women to accumulate political capital. The victory here is performative, not structural, because his subjectivity never changed—he remains a power broker jumping between different narratives.
