FIFA的贪婪与特朗普的共谋：一场关于“定价权”的闭环骗局FIFA's Greed and Trump's Complicity: A Closed-Loop Scam of Pricing Power

国际 结构层 · 文化层 · 元暴力 The Guardian ↗ 2026-07-21 § 链接
体育盛事在权力者眼中并非竞技，而是通过垄断解释权来榨取价值的金融产品。
Major sporting events are not about athletics, but financial products used by power brokers to extract value through monopoly.

特朗普在Fox News上的那句“必须在我还在的时候再次举办”，撕掉了FIFA最后一点关于“足球精神”的遮羞布。这根本不是什么体育赛事，而是一场由顶级权力者主导的、关于定价权的共谋游戏。FIFA通过操纵规则（statutes）和灵活的轮换政策，将世界杯这个全球最大的认知入口，变成了一个可以根据营收预测随时调整地点的金融资产。

这次共谋的逻辑极其简单：FIFA在2026年因为之前的操作失误（卡塔尔冬奥移档）给Fox Sports送了一个廉价的Bargain，导致数十亿美元的潜在利润被Fox截流。现在，FIFA急于在2038年回归美国，本质上是为了通过重新设定TV rights的竞标机制，夺回被抢走的“定价权”。而特朗普则试图将这种商业上的“回归”转化为其个人权力的存在性证明——通过举办盛会来确立其在公共空间中的主导地位。

最令人作呕的是这种元暴力的运作方式：FIFA在处理2034年沙特申办时，用一个极短的注册窗口期直接把澳大利亚等竞争者排除在外。这种“快闪式”的决策流程，让所谓的“成员协会民主”变成了一个巨大的scam。在FIFA总统因凡蒂诺的权力结构里，只有符合资本增值逻辑的路径才是“正确”的，而其他国家的足球发展机会则被视为可以随意牺牲的成本。

当世界杯的门票和款待收入（hospitality revenue）首次超过电视版权，这意味着FIFA已经完成了从“体育组织”到“奢侈品运营商”的身份转换。他们不再关心足球在哪里被热爱，只关心哪里的人愿意支付最高的价格来购买一种“参与感”。在这种共谋中，足球本身被客体化了，它成了权力者在酒店大堂里分赃的筹码。

Trump’s remark on Fox News—that the World Cup must return while he is still around—strips away FIFA’s last shred of "football spirit" facade. This is not a sporting event; it is a game of complicity over pricing power, orchestrated by top-tier power brokers. By manipulating statutes and rotation policies, FIFA has turned the World Cup—the world's largest cognitive entry point—into a financial asset whose location is adjusted based on revenue projections.

The logic of this complicity is simple: FIFA handed Fox Sports a bargain for the 2026 rights due to previous operational failures (the Qatar shift), allowing Fox to intercept billions in potential profit. FIFA’s desperation to return to the US by 2038 is essentially an attempt to reclaim that lost pricing power by resetting the TV rights auction mechanism. Meanwhile, Trump seeks to translate this commercial return into a confirmation of his own existential power—using the spectacle to assert dominance in the public square.

The most repulsive part is the operation of meta-violence: FIFA’s handling of the 2034 Saudi bid, where a truncated registration window effectively erased competitors like Australia. This "flash-bid" process turns the supposed democracy of member associations into a total scam. In Infantino’s power structure, only the path that maximizes capital growth is deemed "correct," while the development of football in other nations is treated as a disposable cost.

As ticketing and hospitality revenue surpass TV rights for the first time, FIFA has completed its identity shift from a "sports body" to a "luxury operator." They no longer care where football is loved; they only care where people will pay the highest premium for a manufactured sense of "belonging." In this web of complicity, football itself is objectified—reduced to a chip for power brokers to gamble with in hotel lobbies.
