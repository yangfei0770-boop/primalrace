核能特权：一场关于“谁是好孩子”的共谋游戏Nuclear Privilege: A Game of Complicity and 'Good Boys'

国际 结构层 · 文化层 · 元暴力 The New York Times ↗ 2026-07-23 § 链接
核不扩散是武器化的叙事，其本质是权力对解释权的垄断。
Non-proliferation is a weaponized narrative; its essence is the monopoly of interpretation by power.

这根本不是什么关于能源或安全的战略布局，而是一次典型的叙事武器化 (weaponized narrative) 操作。美国在帮沙特建核电站的同时摧毁伊朗的核能力，这在逻辑上完全不成立，但在权力博弈中极其高效：它通过定义“合法”与“非法”的核能力，完成了对中东地区权力秩序的重新定价。

所谓的“核不扩散”在此时成了个巨大的 scam。当一个国家被定义为“合作伙伴”时，核能是文明的基建；当另一个国家被定义为“敌对者”时，同样的原子能就是恐怖主义的温床。这种双标正是元暴力 (meta violence) 的体现——由强者垄断了解释权，定义什么是“安全”，从而将结构性暴力伪装成一种维护世界和平的理性选择。

沙特与美国的这次交易是一场典型的共谋 (complicity)。沙特通过出卖地缘忠诚换取核特权，而美国则通过这种“赏赐”来巩固其在地区的支配地位。在这种共谋中，所谓的“国际法”和“安全协议”只是掩体，掩盖的是最原始的权力掠夺：谁能拥有毁灭世界的按钮，取决于谁在白宫的剧本里扮演一个“听话”的角色。

这场博弈的代价永远由那些被排除在叙事之外的底层民众承担。核能的特权被交换，而战争的风险被转嫁。我们看到的不是文明的博弈，而是一群掌握了认知入口的男人，在用原子弹的规模玩一场名为“地缘政治”的权力游戏。

This is not a strategic layout of energy or security, but a classic operation of weaponized narrative. By helping Saudi Arabia build a nuclear program while destroying Iran's, the U.S. proves that nuclear non-proliferation is a massive scam. The logic doesn't hold, but the power play is efficient: it re-prices the power order of the Middle East by defining which nuclear capabilities are "legitimate" and which are "illegal."

This double standard is the embodiment of meta violence. The powerful monopolize the right of interpretation, defining "security" to disguise structural violence as a rational choice for global peace. When a state is a "partner," nuclear energy is civil infrastructure; when it is an "enemy," the same atomic energy is a cradle for terrorism.

The deal between Saudi Arabia and the U.S. is a textbook case of complicity. Saudi Arabia trades geopolitical loyalty for nuclear privilege, while the U.S. consolidates its regional dominance through this "gift." In this complicity, "international law" and "security pacts" are merely shields covering the primal seizure of power: who gets the button to destroy the world depends on who plays the "good boy" in the White House script.

The cost of this game is always borne by the voiceless at the bottom. Nuclear privilege is traded, and the risk of war is transferred. This is not a clash of civilizations, but a group of men controlling the cognitive gateways, playing a power game called "geopolitics" with the scale of atomic bombs.
