囚徒之妻的TikTok：被武器化的浪漫爱与品牌化共谋Prison Wives of TikTok: The Weaponization of Romantic Love and Branded Complicity

性别 文化层 · 结构层 The Guardian ↗ 2026-07-21 § 链接
将受虐的浪漫爱转化为流量资产，是父权结构下的一种自我欺骗式最优解。
Turning masochistic romance into traffic assets is a self-deceptive optimal expression within the patriarchal structure.

《Prison Wives of TikTok》这个节目名本身就是一场巨大的 scam。它把女性与囚犯的浪漫关系包装成一种“近期发展”的社会现象，实际上不过是将传统的 romantic love 陷阱数字化、品牌化。这些女性在社交媒体上广播她们与狱中男性的关系，试图通过构建一个“深情等待”或“救赎者”的人设来获取注意力资源。这在本质上是一次典型的表达武器化：她们试图夺取认知入口，将一段极不对等的、基于生物墙和结构性禁锢的关系，通过 TikTok 的滤镜转化为 money-making power。

在这种叙事里，女性在潜意识中与父权结构达成了共谋。她们通过扮演一个被认可的、具有牺牲精神的女性角色，在一个极低门槛的细分领域寻找自己的“最优解表达”。但这种最优解是假的，因为它的燃料是内化了的自我贬低。当她们在讨论如何通过这段关系进行 brand-building 时，她们实际上是在将自己的主体性物化为一种可消费的商品，以换取在虚拟空间里的微小权力感。

最讽刺的是，这种“品牌建设”依然在男本位叙事的闭环中运行。无论她们如何通过 TikTok 获得流量，其核心依然是围绕着那个被囚禁的男性展开。这不过是把私人的受虐体验搬到了公共空间，让大众在围观中完成一次集体性的文化暴力。这种所谓的“社区”并非真正的团结，而是一群掉进同一个浪漫陷阱的病友在互相确认对方的枷锁是否足够沉重。

The title "Prison Wives of TikTok" is a massive scam. It packages romantic relationships between women and prisoners as a "recent development," but in reality, it is merely the digitalization and branding of the classic romantic love trap. By broadcasting their affairs on social media, these women attempt to seize cognitive entry points, transforming an extremely asymmetrical relationship—rooted in biological walls and structural confinement—into money-making power via TikTok filters.

In this narrative, women enter into a subconscious complicity with the patriarchal structure. By performing the role of a recognized, self-sacrificing woman, they seek a "fake optimal expression" in a low-barrier niche. This is not a true optimal expression because its fuel is internalized self-deprecation. As they discuss brand-building around these relationships, they are objectifying their own subjectivity into a consumable commodity in exchange for a marginal sense of power in virtual spaces.

Most ironically, this brand-building still operates within a masculine-centric narrative. No matter how much traffic they generate, the core remains centered on the imprisoned male. It is simply the migration of private masochism into the public sphere, allowing the masses to perform cultural violence through spectatorship. This so-called "community" is not true solidarity; it is a group of patients who have fallen into the same romantic trap, mutually confirming that their chains are sufficiently heavy.
