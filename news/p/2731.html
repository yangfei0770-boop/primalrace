用“安保经费”掩盖的结构性抛弃Structural Abandonment Masked as 'Security Funding'

国际 结构层 · 文化层 · 元暴力 The Guardian ↗ 2026-07-22 § 链接
把受害者变成申请资助的“乙方”，是典型的结构性暴力 PR。
Turning victims into 'applicants' for aid is a classic PR move for structural violence.

每五天一次袭击，但政府给出的解法是：请提交一份证据充分、风险描述清晰的申请书，然后等待 18 个月的审核期。这简直是一个完美的 scam。政府通过宣布 4000 万英镑的“保护计划”在 cultural 层面上完成了“我们很关心”的叙事，但在 structural 层面上，它通过设立极高的准入门槛和低效的审批流程，将救济变成了某种需要竞争的资源。

这种机制极其阴险：它要求受害者必须证明自己“足够可怜”且“足够专业”地描述风险，才能获得保护。如果申请被拒且没有解释，那么在官僚系统的逻辑里，这次袭击就成了“证据不足”的非事件。这不仅是行政低效，而是一种元暴力的延伸——定义谁值得被保护的权力依然在那个男性中心、白人至上的统治结构手中。

最讽刺的是，当 1/6 的英国人认为穆斯林人口是“基础性威胁”时，这种仇恨已经在文化层面上被 normalise 了。而政府提供的 CCTV 和围栏，本质上是在告诉受害者：世界已经敌对，请自行在自己的笼子里寻求安全。这根本不是在消除暴力，而是在通过给受害者发“安保礼包”来掩盖它在引导仇恨叙事上的共谋与失职。

An attack every five days, yet the government's solution is: please submit a well-evidenced, clearly articulated risk assessment and wait 18 months for approval. It is a complete scam. By announcing a £40m 'protective scheme,' the state performs a narrative of care at the cultural level, while at the structural level, it transforms relief into a competitive resource through prohibitive barriers and bureaucratic inertia.

This mechanism is sinister. It demands that victims prove they are 'miserable enough' and 'professional enough' to describe their trauma to earn protection. If a request is denied without explanation, the attack is effectively erased as a 'non-event' due to insufficient evidence. This is not mere inefficiency; it is an extension of meta-violence—the power to define who is worthy of protection remains firmly within the masculine-centric, white-supremacist ruling structure.

Most ironic is that while 1/6 of Britons view the Muslim population as a 'foundational threat,' this hatred has been normalized at the cultural level. The government's offering of CCTV and fences is essentially telling victims: the world is now hostile, please find safety within your own cage. This is not eliminating violence; it is using 'security gift bags' to mask their complicity in the failure to dismantle the hate narrative.
