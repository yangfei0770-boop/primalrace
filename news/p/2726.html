核协议的伪装与原初种族的血祭The Masquerade of Nuclear Deals and the Blood Sacrifice of the Primal Race

国际 结构层 · 文化层 · 元暴力 The Guardian ↗ 2026-07-22 § 链接
用“和平”包装的核扩散，本质是权势者在共谋场域中对底层生命的定价权博弈。
A 'peaceful' nuclear deal is a weaponized narrative where power elites gamble with the lives of the disposable.

所谓的“和平核合作协议” (peaceful nuclear cooperation agreement) 是一场典型的叙事武器化 scam。美国一方面以防止伊朗浓缩铀为由发动战争，将无数士兵和平民推向直接暴力的 direct layer；另一方面却给沙特开绿灯，允许其浓缩铀。这种逻辑上的自相矛盾，揭露了“非扩散” (nonproliferation) 只是一个被武器化的认知入口，其真实功能是根据政治盟友的身份来定义什么是“安全”，什么是“威胁”。

在这场大国博弈的结构层 (structural layer) 之下，是极其残酷的共谋机制。特朗普政府与沙特王室的共谋，是用数以十亿计的商业合同和战略利益，换取对核技术的解释权。而这种共谋的代价，则由那些在战争中被击毙的士兵（如 1Lt Tyler Feehan）和被 ICE 追杀至死的移民（如 Juan Jairo Coronilla Durán）来支付。在元暴力 (meta violence) 的逻辑里，这些生命被降格为可消耗的工具，他们的死亡被包装成“必要的牺牲”或“意外”，从而在文化层 (cultural layer) 维持了权势者的正当性。

最讽刺的共谋发生在对内治理上：一边是核协议带来的巨额资本流动，一边是 CDC 资金被削减导致麻疹病例 35 年来最高。当公共卫生系统被拆解，当底层民众在生物墙前失去最基本的医疗保障，权势者却在通过“Trump accounts”这种表演性让步来收买人心。这不过是将资源从结构性保障转移到个人化施舍的把戏，旨在让弱势者在依赖中完成自我规训。

这场存在性战争的赢家永远是那些掌握定价权的人。他们定义什么是“文明”，什么是“安全”，然后在这个定义之下，合法地执行最原始的种族剥削和性别/阶级暴力。当核武器的钥匙在共谋者手中传递，而底层的疫苗在仓库中失效，这本身就是一种巨大的、弥散的结构性暴力。

The so-called “peaceful nuclear cooperation agreement” is a classic scam of weaponized expression. The US administration justifies war against Iran to prevent uranium enrichment while simultaneously granting Saudi Arabia the same capability. This blatant contradiction reveals that “nonproliferation” is merely a weaponized cognitive entry point; its real function is to define “security” or “threat” based on the identity of political allies.

Beneath the structural layer of this geopolitical game lies a brutal mechanism of complicity. The alliance between the Trump administration and the Saudi royalty trades multi-billion-dollar commercial contracts for the monopoly on the interpretation of nuclear technology. The cost of this complicity is paid by those thrust into the direct layer of violence: soldiers like 1Lt Tyler Feehan and immigrants like Juan Jairo Coronilla Durán, hunted to death by ICE. Under the logic of meta-violence, these lives are downgraded to consumable tools, their deaths packaged as “necessary sacrifices” or “accidents” to maintain the legitimacy of the powerful at the cultural layer.

The most cynical complicity occurs domestically: while nuclear deals facilitate massive capital flow, CDC funding is decimated, leading to a 35-year high in measles cases. As the public health system is dismantled and the marginalized lose basic biological protections, the elites perform “generosity” through “Trump accounts.” This is a calculated move to shift resources from structural safeguards to individual patronage, ensuring the weak remain in a state of dependent self-discipline.

In this existential war, the winners are always those who hold the pricing power. They define “civilization” and “security,” and under these definitions, they legitimately execute the most primitive racial, gender, and class violence. When the keys to nuclear weapons are passed between co-conspirators while vaccines for the poor expire in warehouses, we are witnessing a massive, diffused form of structural violence.
