死亡计划：一场关于主体性最后的夺回战Death Planning: The Final Reclamation of Subjectivity

哲学 结构层 · 文化层 The Guardian ↗ 2026-07-17 § 链接
提前医疗指示不是法律文件，而是个体在生物墙坍塌前对主体性的最后一次确证。
An Advance Care Directive is not a legal form, but the final assertion of subjectivity before the biological wall collapses.

这篇关于“临终房间”的叙事，表面在讨论医疗规划，本质上是在讨论存在性战争的终局。当一个人进入昏迷、失去表达能力时，他实际上已经撞上了最残酷的生物墙。此时，如果缺乏一个具有法律效力的 Advance Care Directive (ACD)，这个人的主体性就彻底死亡了，剩下的只有被共谋者们——医生、家属、制度——定义的“生存状态”。

在没有 ACD 的情况下，死亡变成了家属之间的博弈场。正如文中提到的，子女之间会因为“要不要插管”而激烈争吵。这种争吵极其讽刺：在病人已经无法表达的真空地带，共谋者们通过争夺解释权来获得某种心理补偿，而病人本身却成了被他们争夺的客体。在这种结构性暴力中，一个人的死亡方式竟然取决于他家庭关系的和谐程度，这简直是荒诞的 scam。

一个有效的 AHD/ACD 实际上是一次“表达的武器化”：它通过预先书写，在未来的权力真空中建立起一道防御墙，防止自己的身体被医疗体制和家属的感性冲动所掠夺。当父亲在文中拒绝“生命维持治疗”时，他实际上是在执行一个真.最优解表达——拒绝在毫无主体性的状态下被机械性地维持生物指标，从而保障了自己作为人的尊严。

但我们要警惕这种“好新闻”背后的结构性缺失。文中提到仅有 6% 的澳大利亚人完成了正式的 ACD。这意味着绝大多数人在面对死亡时，依然处于一种被动地被定义、被处置的状态。人权即女权，而人权的核心就是对自身身体的绝对处置权。如果一个人在生命最后时刻的表达权都被剥夺，那么他之前的所有存在性博弈都将毫无意义。

This narrative of the 'dying room' ostensibly discusses medical planning, but it is fundamentally about the endgame of the Existential War. When a person falls into a coma and loses the ability to express, they hit the most brutal biological wall. Without a legally binding ACD, their subjectivity dies instantly, leaving only a 'state of existence' defined by complicitors—doctors, family, and the system.

Without an ACD, death becomes a gambling field for family members. The irony is sharp: in the vacuum of the patient's voice, complicitors fight over the right of interpretation to achieve psychological closure, while the patient is reduced to a mere object. In this structural violence, the way a person dies depends on the harmony of their family dynamics—a complete scam.

An effective AHD/ACD is a form of the weaponisation of expression. By writing in advance, one builds a defensive wall in the future power vacuum, preventing their body from being plundered by medical regimes or the emotional impulses of kin. By refusing 'life-sustaining treatment,' the father in the story executed a true optimal expression—refusing to be mechanically maintained as a biological specimen without subjectivity.

However, we must be wary of the structural void behind this 'good news.' With only 6% of Australians completing formal ACDs, the vast majority remain passive objects to be defined and disposed of at the end. Human rights are women's rights, and the core of human rights is absolute bodily autonomy. If the right to express oneself at the final moment is stripped away, all previous existential gambles become meaningless.
