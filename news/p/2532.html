Natural disasters are not the violence; the disparity in survival capacity is.

面对 7.3 级地震，人们习惯性地将其归类为“自然灾害”。但在加尔通的暴力三角中，地震本身是物理现象，而由此产生的恐惧与潜在伤亡则是 structural violence 的结果。当墨西哥和危地马拉的民众在社交媒体上拍摄摇晃的建筑和山体滑坡时，他们面对的不仅是地壳运动，更是长期被低估的建筑标准和资源分配不均带来的生存博弈。

注意新闻中的细节：美国 USGS 预测海浪可能高出 1 米，而墨西哥海军秘书则试图将其修正为 0.5 米。这种对数据的“微调”并非科学探讨，而是一种典型的认知入口争夺。通过降低风险预期，统治者在试图通过文化层面的叙事来掩盖结构层面的准备不足，从而将潜在的恐慌转化为一种“可控”的假象。这是一种潜意识的共谋：政府通过淡化危机来维持秩序，而民众在缺乏独立预警能力的绝望中，被迫接受这种被阉割的真相。

在这种生存博弈中，最底层的受害者永远是那些无法通过物理迁移逃离风险地带的人。当总统在新闻稿中宣布“暂无重大损失”时，这种叙事抹平了那些在山体滑坡中失去家园、但在统计数据中被忽略的边缘个体。Potential（本可达到的安全状态）与 Actual（简陋的避难所）之间的差额，正是被掩盖的暴力。

Facing a 7.3 magnitude earthquake, the public instinctively categorizes it as a "natural disaster." However, within Galtung's Violence Triangle, the quake is a physical event; the resulting terror and potential casualties are the products of structural violence. As people in Mexico and Guatemala film swaying buildings and landslides, they are not just battling tectonic shifts, but a fierce existential game played against substandard construction and systemic resource inequity.

Notice the nuance: the US USGS predicted waves up to 1 meter, while the Mexican Secretary of the Navy attempted to revise this to 0.5 meters. This "fine-tuning" of data is not scientific discourse, but a struggle for the cognitive entry point. By downplaying the risk, the state uses cultural narrative to mask structural inadequacy, transforming potential panic into a facade of "controllability." This is a form of complicity: the government maintains order through minimization, while a populace lacking independent warning systems is forced to accept a castrated truth.

In this survival game, the primary victims are those unable to execute a physical migration away from risk zones. When the President declares "no major damage" in a press release, the narrative erases the marginal individuals who lost their homes to landslides but remain invisible in the statistics. The gap between the Potential (the safety that should have been) and the Actual (shabby shelters) is precisely where the hidden violence resides.
