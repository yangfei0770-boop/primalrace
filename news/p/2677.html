核燃料的入场券与男权共谋的生意经Nuclear Fuel Tickets and the Business of Masculine Complicity

国际 结构层 · 文化层 · 元暴力 The New York Times ↗ 2026-07-22 § 链接
文明的核协议本质上是男性权力中心在交换对暴力的定义权。
Civilized nuclear accords are essentially masculine power centers trading the right to define violence.

这不过是一场典型的男性中心叙事 (masculine-centric narrative) 闭环交易。美国政府与沙特王室在谈论什么？是核燃料浓缩、是 Westinghouse 的数十亿美金订单、是地缘政治的 bind。在这套叙事里，核能被冠以“民用”之名，但所有参与博弈的男性权力者都心知肚明：浓缩能力就是潜在的直接暴力 (direct violence) 筹码。所谓的“担心核武器”不过是博弈中的讨价还价，而非真正的道德底线。

这场交易是极高层级的共谋 (complicity)。特朗普政府将核合作与沙特承认以色列的政治条件解绑，这意味着美国在用一个潜在的全球性安全风险换取短期内核工业的经济利益和对沙特政权的深度绑定。在这种结构性暴力 (structural violence) 面前，所谓的“国际安全”只是一个被武器化的词汇，用来掩盖资本与权力在私下达成的分赃协议。

最讽刺的是，这种“大国博弈”的逻辑与父权制在私域的运作如出一辙：通过掌控核心资源（解释权、定价权、核燃料），在一个由男性定义的封闭圈层里，通过互换利益来维持一个看似稳定的秩序。在这个秩序中，被牺牲的永远是那些不在谈判桌上的群体——无论是中东的普通民众，还是被这些宏大叙事掩盖的女性生存现状。他们不需要在乎谁会被核辐射或战争波及，因为在元暴力 (meta violence) 的逻辑里，只有掌握了“制造可能性”的人才被视为真正的人。

This is a textbook closed-loop transaction of masculine-centric narrative. What are the U.S. government and the Saudi royalty discussing? Nuclear fuel enrichment, billions in orders for Westinghouse, and geopolitical binding. In this narrative, nuclear energy is labeled as "civilian," but every male power-player in the game knows: enrichment capability is the chip for potential direct violence. The "fear of nuclear weapons" is merely a bargaining chip in the game, not a genuine moral baseline.

This accord is a high-level manifestation of complicity. By decoupling nuclear cooperation from the demand for Saudi recognition of Israel, the Trump administration is trading a potential global security risk for short-term economic gains in the nuclear industry and a deeper bind with the Saudi regime. In the face of such structural violence, "international security" is just a weaponized term used to mask the spoils-sharing agreement reached in private between capital and power.

The irony is that this "great power game" logic mirrors the operation of patriarchy in the private sphere: controlling core resources (the right to interpret, the right to price, nuclear fuel) to maintain a seemingly stable order within a closed circle defined by men. In this order, those not at the negotiating table—whether the ordinary people of the Middle East or the female existence erased by these grand narratives—are always the ones sacrificed. They don't care who gets hit by radiation or war, because in the logic of meta violence, only those who hold the power to "manufacture possibilities" are seen as truly human.
