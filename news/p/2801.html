用“稳定”定义驱逐：一场关于生存权的叙事诈骗Defining 'Stability' as Deportation: A Narrative Scam of Survival

国际 文化层 · 结构层 · 直接层 The New York Times ↗ 2026-07-24 § 链接
当权力通过重新定义“稳定”来剥夺生存权时，这就是典型的文化暴力。
When power redefines 'stability' to strip away the right to exist, it is pure cultural violence.

这篇评论揭露了一个极其恶毒的逻辑悖论：美国政府一方面通过 State Department 宣布海地是 Level 4 的“禁区”，另一方面却在法律层面上宣布海地已经“足够稳定”，足以让 33 万海地人被驱逐回那个地狱。

这就是典型的 Weaponized Expression。权力在这里不关心海地是否真的稳定，它关心的是如何通过定义“稳定”这个词，来合法化一次大规模的人口清洗。在 Violence = Potential − Actual 的公式里，这 33 万人的 Actual 状态正被强行从一个有法治保障的社会拉向一个由帮派统治的崩溃地带。这种差额就是最直接的暴力。

更深层的 Meta Violence 在于，美国及其伙伴在海地扮演了长期的“共谋者”。他们通过派遣资源不足的肯尼亚特遣队、实施碎片化的制裁，制造了一种“我们在尝试救助”的文化掩体。这种掩体让国际社会在心理上完成了自我宽慰，而实际上却在维持一种结构性暴力：确保海地永远处于一种“需要被管理但永远无法自洽”的半崩溃状态。

所谓的“人道主义干预”在这里成了最廉价的 Scam。它不是为了建立海地的机构能力，而是为了在不承担责任的前提下，维持一个可以随时被定义为“不稳定”或“稳定”的殖民地样板。当这些人被驱逐回那个 90% 被帮派控制的首都时，他们面对的不是家乡，而是一个被西方政策亲手喂养出来的战争机器。

This piece exposes a malicious logical paradox: the US government maintains a Level 4 'Do Not Travel' advisory for Haiti while simultaneously arguing in court that Haiti is 'stable enough' to justify the mass deportation of 330,000 people.

This is a textbook case of Weaponized Expression. The power structure doesn't care about the actual stability of Haiti; it cares about how to redefine the word 'stability' to legitimize a mass cleansing. In the formula Violence = Potential − Actual, the Actual state of these 330,000 people is being forcibly dragged from a society with legal protections into a gang-ruled wasteland. This delta is direct violence.

The deeper Meta Violence lies in the role of the US and its partners as long-term complicitors. By deploying under-resourced Kenyan forces and piecemeal sanctions, they created a cultural smokescreen of 'trying to help.' This allows the international community to feel a sense of moral relief while maintaining a structural violence that ensures Haiti remains in a state of 'managed collapse'—perpetually broken but always under a perceived need for external management.

The so-called 'humanitarian intervention' is the ultimate scam. It is not about building institutional capacity, but about maintaining a colonial laboratory that can be defined as 'unstable' or 'stable' at the whim of the hegemon. When these people are banished back to a capital where 90% of the land is controlled by gangs, they aren't returning home; they are being fed into a war machine manufactured by Western policy.
