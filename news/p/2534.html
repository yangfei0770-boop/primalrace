The 'establishment' halo is merely a cultural shield designed to legitimize structural violence.

Jeffrey Donaldson 的上诉不过是权力者在失去定价权后的最后一次博弈。一个曾经主导北爱尔兰联合派、在西敏斯特扮演关键角色的 establishment figure，在 30 年间将儿童视作性客体，而他维持的“敬畏神且禁酒”的形象，正是典型的 weaponized expression。他通过塑造一个符合宗教与道德期待的身份，成功在文化层建立了防御墙，使得他在实施 direct violence 的同时，依然能持有最高级别的社会信用。

最令人作呕的共谋在于其妻子 Eleanor Donaldson。尽管她被判定为精神不适，但“协助与教唆”的结论揭示了一个残酷的结构：在元暴力的逻辑下，共谋者通过在私人领域维护施暴者的权力，来换取自身在结构中的安全感。这种共谋不仅是情感的，更是政治性的——当一个男人成为权力中心，他的伴侣往往成为维持这个中心不坍塌的共谋零件。

现在 DUP 内部开始爆料他其实是个“酗酒的性骚扰者”，这种所谓的“反思”更像是一场表演性的切割。当一个人的权力-信用体系崩溃，曾经的共谋者会迅速将之前的沉默转化为现在的“正义”，以确保自己不被卷入坍塌的废墟。这种从“神化”到“妖魔化”的快速切换，恰恰证明了之前的“神圣性”完全是人为制造的 scam。

Jeffrey Donaldson's appeal is nothing more than a final gamble by a power-player who has lost his pricing power. As a former DUP leader and an establishment figure in Westminster, he treated children as sexual objects for 30 years while maintaining a meticulously crafted image of a 'God-fearing teetotaller.' This was a classic case of weaponized expression: by performing a role that met religious and moral expectations, he built a cultural wall that allowed him to commit direct violence while retaining maximum social credit.

The most repulsive part of this complicity is the role of his wife, Eleanor Donaldson. Though deemed unfit for trial, the finding that she 'aided and abetted' reveals a brutal structural truth: under the logic of meta-violence, co-conspirators maintain the predator's power in the private sphere to secure their own position within the structure. This complicity is not just emotional, but political—when a man becomes the center of power, his partner often becomes a functional component keeping that center from collapsing.

The sudden surge of claims from senior DUP figures calling him a 'drunken sex pest' is a performative decoupling. When a person's power-credit system crashes, former co-conspirators swiftly convert their previous silence into 'justice' to avoid being buried in the ruins. This rapid swing from 'divinity' to 'demonization' only proves that the original 'sanctity' was a complete scam.
