边境的烟雾与被抹除的肉身Border Smoke and the Erased Flesh

国际 直接层 · 结构层 · 文化层 · 元暴力 The New York Times ↗ 2026-07-23 § 链接
战争叙事通过将肉体客体化为“坐标”，掩盖其本质上的性别与权力暴力。
War narratives objectify human bodies into 'coordinates' to mask the essence of gendered and power violence.

又是典型的战争叙事：美军的 airstrikes，伊朗的 retaliatory strike，以及各种 border crossing 的坐标。在这些宏大叙事的博弈中，人的存在被极度简化为“two people killed”或“no casualties”。这种表达方式本身就是一种 weaponization，它将复杂的生存状态抽干，只剩下军事目标的损益表。

注意看那些被验证的视频：人们拎着 suitcases 和 rucksacks 在爆炸中奔逃。这些行李代表的是个体试图在结构性暴力中寻找生存最优解的绝望尝试——物理迁移。但无论他们迁往何处，只要他们处于男性中心叙事（masculine-centric narrative）构建的战争机器中，他们就永远是可被消耗的客体。这种暴力不仅是 direct 的爆炸，更是 structural 的，因为决定谁在哪个坐标被炸死的人，永远是坐在指挥室里的男性。

这次袭击中，被摧毁的 telecommunications tower 恰恰是个隐喻：在元暴力的统治下，被统治者的表达权被首先切断。而外界在讨论“敌对无人机”和“军事设施”时，那些在烟雾中奔逃的女性、儿童及其生存权，被自动过滤成了背景噪音。人权在此时成了最廉价的叙事入口，而真正的暴力则在这些“战略目标”的掩护下，继续对原初种族进行着无声的殖民。

Another classic war narrative: U.S. airstrikes, Iranian retaliatory strikes, and the coordinates of various border crossings. In the gamble of these grand narratives, human existence is reduced to "two people killed" or "no casualties." This mode of expression is a form of weaponization; it drains the complexity of survival, leaving only a profit-and-loss statement of military objectives.

Look at the verified videos: people fleeing with suitcases and rucksacks amidst explosions. These belongings represent a desperate attempt by individuals to find an optimal expression of survival through physical migration within structural violence. Yet, no matter where they move, as long as they exist within the machinery of war built on masculine-centric narrative, they remain disposable objects. This violence is not just direct explosions, but structural, because those deciding who dies at which coordinate are always men in command centers.

The destroyed telecommunications tower in this attack is a perfect metaphor: under meta-violence, the expression rights of the oppressed are the first to be severed. While the world discusses "hostile drones" and "military installations," the women, children, and their right to exist—fleeing through the smoke—are automatically filtered out as background noise. Human rights become a cheap narrative entry point, while the real violence continues its silent colonization of the Primal Race, shielded by the term "strategic targets."
