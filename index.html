原初种族

The Primal Race

作者 / Author  ·  费扬  Yang Fei

数据 / Data  ·  Claude

邮箱 / Email  ·  yang.fei0770@gmail.com

本书基于约翰·加尔通的"暴力三角"框架，提出"不公即暴力"的公理化定义，并构建"原初种族理论"——认为生理性别本质上是一个种族问题。女性作为人类历史上被殖民的"原初种族"，遭受了系统性的生物学改变与掠夺，其形成逻辑为后续所有种族构成和暴力提供了蓝图。

Built on Johan Galtung's Violence Triangle, this book axiomatizes "injustice is violence" and constructs the Primal Race Theory — that biological sex is, at its root, a race question. Women, as humanity's original colonized race, have been subjected to systemic biological alteration and extraction; their formation logic became the blueprint for every race construct and every act of violence that followed.
